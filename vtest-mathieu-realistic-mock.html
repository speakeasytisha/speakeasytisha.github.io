Published timing guide

Use this as the pacing reference: the real VTest is adaptive, so the total test length can vary. These timings are the public per-task ranges published by VTest and are the best benchmark for training.

Listening + Reading

  • Read and answer: read 45–120 sec, answer 45–75 sec
  • Listen and answer: listen 45–60 sec, answer 45–60 sec
  • Listen and choose: 40–75 sec
  • Multiple choice: 30 sec
  • Fill in the blank: 75 sec

Speaking

  • Reading fluency: 60 sec
  • Listen and repeat: 15 sec
  • Speaking proficiency: prep 40–60 sec, speak 45–90 sec

Writing

  • Writing task: 30 minutes
  • The prompt may be an essay-style response or a photo description
  • This mock keeps model answers visible for training, even though the real exam does not
