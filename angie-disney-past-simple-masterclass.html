⭐ Grammar Step 1 — When do we use the past simple?

Use the past simple for finished actions in the past. The action is finished and we often know when it happened.

Finished action

I worked last summer.

Past experience

I helped customers at the market.

Past travel

I travelled alone last year.

Past interview

I had an interview in October.

Past Simple signal words: yesterday • last week • last month • last year • two days ago • in 2024 • when I was younger • during the summer • this morning

🪄 Grammar Step 2 — Affirmative form

Structure

Subject + past simple verb

I worked.

She visited Disneyland Paris.

Pronouns

I worked / You worked / He worked / She worked / We worked / They worked

Regular verbs do not change with he/she/it in past simple.

Regular verbs

Add -ed.

work → worked

Verb ending in -e

Add -d.

live → lived

Consonant + y

Change y → ied.

study → studied

Irregular verbs

Learn the special form.

go → went / have → had

Choose the correct affirmative form

❌ Grammar Step 3 — Negative form

Structure

Subject + did not + base verb

I did not work yesterday.

She didn’t visit the park.

Very important

After didn’t, use the base verb.

❌ I didn’t worked.

✅ I didn’t work.

Choose the correct negative form

❓ Grammar Step 4 — Questions

Yes / No questions

Did + subject + base verb?

Did you work last summer?

Did she travel alone?

WH questions

WH + did + subject + base verb?

Where did you work?

What did you learn?

Choose the correct question

🧭 Past Simple Method — 3 steps

1
Sentence type
positive / negative / question
2
Use DID for negative & questions
didn’t / Did…?
3
After DID, use base verb
not went → go
4
Add a past time expression
last week / ago / in 2024

📅 Time Expressions — ago, last, in, during, for

last + time

last week / last month / last year

ago

two days ago / three years ago

in + year/month

in 2024 / in July

for + duration

for three months / for two weeks

“Since” comes later with present perfect.

🎤 Disney & VTest Interview Practice

Use the past simple to talk about real experiences.

🟢 A1/A2

I worked at a market. I helped customers. I learned new words.

🟡 A2+/B1

Last summer, I worked at a market. I helped customers, sold products and learned how to communicate more clearly.

🟣 B1

During my last work experience, I helped customers, answered questions and stayed calm when the shop was busy.
