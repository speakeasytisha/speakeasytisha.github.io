🧱 Grammar lane: one tense at a time

🟦 Present simple

Use it for your job, routine, and facts.

  • Affirmative: I organise weddings.
  • Negative: I do not organise corporate events every week.
  • Question: Do you work with florists?

subject + base verb

subject + do / does not + base verb

Do / Does + subject + base verb?

Routine, travail, habitudes.

🟨 Present continuous

Use it for what is happening now or around now.

  • Affirmative: I am preparing the venue.
  • Negative: I am not answering emails right now.
  • Question: Are you checking the schedule?

subject + am / is / are + verb-ing

subject + am / is / are not + verb-ing

Am / Is / Are + subject + verb-ing?

Maintenant, en ce moment.

🟧 Past simple

Use it for finished actions in the past.

  • Affirmative: I organised a salon in Paris.
  • Negative: I did not forget the suppliers.
  • Question: Did you meet new clients?

subject + past simple

subject + did not + base verb

Did + subject + base verb?

Action terminée dans le passé.

🟩 Future: be going to

Use it for a plan or intention.

  • Affirmative: I am going to prepare a new event.
  • Negative: I am not going to change the venue.
  • Question: Are you going to call the florist?

subject + am / is / are going to + base verb

subject + am / is / are not going to + base verb

Am / Is / Are + subject + going to + base verb?

Projet, intention, plan.

📞 Phone dialogue

Client: Hello Sabine, this is Madame Laurent calling. Are you available for a quick question?

Bonjour Sabine, c’est Madame Laurent à l’appareil. Êtes-vous disponible pour une question rapide ?

Sabine: Hello Madame Laurent, yes, of course. How can I help you?

Bonjour Madame Laurent, oui, bien sûr. Comment puis-je vous aider ?

Client: We need to change the timing for the venue visit.

Nous devons changer l’horaire de la visite du lieu.

Sabine: No problem. I am checking the schedule now. Yesterday, I confirmed the original time, but I am going to contact the venue again today.

Pas de problème. Je vérifie le planning maintenant. Hier, j’ai confirmé l’horaire initial, mais je vais recontacter le lieu aujourd’hui.

🎭 Realistic roleplay

Situation: You are speaking with an examiner. Explain your job, talk about the salon in Paris, and say what you are going to do next.

  • Who are you?
  • What do you usually do?
  • What happened in Paris?
  • What are you going to do next?
Show model answer

I work as a wedding planner. I usually organise weddings and events, coordinate suppliers, and answer clients. Last week, I organised a salon in Paris. During the event, I met suppliers, welcomed visitors, and answered questions. It was a good professional experience because I learned useful things and found new contacts. Next week, I am going to follow up with suppliers and prepare future projects.
