A

GRAMMAR WORKSHOP A

Would you like…?

Would you like + noun / to + verb is a polite way to offer something or ask a polite question.

C’est une manière polie de proposer quelque chose ou de poser une question.

Would you like+a nounorto + verb
B

GRAMMAR WORKSHOP B

Can I offer you…? / Do you need…?

Can I offer you + noun? proposes a service. Do you need + noun? checks a passenger’s need.

Can I offer you… propose un service. Do you need… vérifie un besoin.

OFFER

Can I offer you some tea?

CHECK A NEED

Do you need a blanket?

C

GRAMMAR WORKSHOP C

Here is… / Here are…

Use Here is for one thing. Use Here are for two or more things.

Here is = une chose. Here are = plusieurs choses.

1 thingHere is your blanket.2+ thingsHere are your headphones.

GRAMMAR WORKSHOP D

Need / would like / would prefer

Passengers use these phrases to say what they need, want, or prefer.

Les passagers utilisent ces expressions pour dire ce dont ils ont besoin, ce qu’ils souhaitent ou ce qu’ils préfèrent.

NEED

I need water.

important / necessary
WOULD LIKE

I would like tea.

polite request
WOULD PREFER

I would prefer an aisle seat.

choice between options
