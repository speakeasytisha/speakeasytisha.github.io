Passenger Requests & Clarification ✈️

A structured lesson to help you identify the passenger’s request, ask better follow-up questions, reformulate clearly, and give a short professional response for the LILATE exam.

Une leçon structurée pour vous aider à identifier la demande du passager, poser de meilleures questions, reformuler clairement et donner une réponse professionnelle courte pour le LILATE.

1) Identify
Find the passenger’s problem and key details.
2) Clarify
Use professional question patterns to check meaning.
3) Respond
Give a short, clear, professional answer.

1) What you need for this part of the LILATE

In Part 1 and Part 2, you need to understand the passenger’s request, ask simple questions, and reformulate clearly.

Dans les parties 1 et 2, vous devez comprendre la demande du passager, poser des questions simples et reformuler clairement.

Key actions

  • Identify the request
  • Ask a follow-up question
  • Reformulate professionally
  • Give useful information
