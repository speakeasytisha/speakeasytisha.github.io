🪄 Lesson 3 — CAN, MAY, COULD, SHOULD

CAN

Use: ability / offer / possibility

Can I help you?

MAY

Use: very polite / formal permission

May I see your ticket?

COULD

Use: polite request / soft question

Could you repeat, please?

SHOULD

Use: advice / recommendation

You should check the app.

Important structure

Modal + base verb

I can help. / May I see? / Could you repeat? / You should wait.

Do not add TO

❌ I can to help you.

✅ I can help you.

❌ You should to wait here.

✅ You should wait here.
