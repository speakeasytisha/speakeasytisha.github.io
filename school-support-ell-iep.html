IEP (IDEA)
  • For eligible students with a disability who need special education.
  • Includes goals, services, and accommodations; reviewed regularly.
  • Starts with a formal evaluation (with parent consent).
504 Plan
  • For students with a disability who need accommodations to access school.
  • No ‚Äúspecial education‚Äù requirement (different law).
  • Often used for medical needs, ADHD, anxiety, etc. (depends on needs).
MTSS / RTI
  • A support system for any student who needs extra help (reading, math, behavior).
  • Often short cycles of small‚Äëgroup support and progress monitoring.
  • Can happen before or alongside evaluations.
ELL/ML support
  • Language program (ESL, co‚Äëteaching, sheltered instruction, etc.).
  • Based on language screening and proficiency levels.
  • Goal: access content + build English proficiency.
