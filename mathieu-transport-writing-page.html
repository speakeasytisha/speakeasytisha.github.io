Writing task

What are the most important things to organise before a business or study trip?

You chose relevant priorities and gave a clear reason for each one. That is a very good foundation for this kind of task.

Your original answer

I would say that the accommodation and transport are the most important things. You have to book your flight or train earlier to avoid the stress of looking for the ticket at the last minute. It is very important to have an accommodation near your workplace or study place. This matters because it saves time and prevent stress. For example, if your accommodation is 1H to your workplace or meeting location, there is a risk to running late, and that's one of the causes of stress. In conclusion, once all your documents are ready, transport and accommodation have to be booked early to avoid last-minute issues and permit to be focus on the purpose of the trip.

Corrected version

I would say that accommodation and transport are the most important things to organise before a business or study trip. You have to book your flight or train early to avoid the stress of looking for tickets at the last minute. It is also very important to have accommodation near your workplace or place of study. This matters because it saves time and prevents stress. For example, if your accommodation is one hour away from your workplace or meeting location, there is a risk of arriving late, and that is one of the main causes of stress. In conclusion, once all your documents are ready, transport and accommodation should be booked early to avoid last-minute problems and allow you to focus on the purpose of the trip.

Upgrade your answer step by step

Clear improvement steps

  1. Start with a direct answer: I think the most important things to organise before a business or study trip are transport and accommodation.
  2. Give two clear reasons: they help you save time and reduce stress.
  3. Add a practical example: if your hotel is far from your meeting place, you may arrive late and feel tired or stressed.
  4. Add one more useful point: documents, schedule, or important contact details can make the answer even stronger.
  5. Finish with a strong conclusion: good organisation makes the trip easier, more efficient, and less stressful.

Stronger model answer

In my opinion, the most important things to organise before a business or study trip are transport, accommodation, and travel documents. First of all, booking your train or flight in advance helps you avoid last-minute stress and often saves money. It is also important to choose accommodation near your workplace, university, or meeting location, because this saves time and makes the trip more comfortable. For example, if your hotel is far from where you need to go every day, you may spend too much time travelling and risk arriving late. In addition, checking your documents, schedule, and important contact details before leaving can help you avoid unnecessary problems. In conclusion, good organisation before a trip helps you stay focused, reduce stress, and make the most of your travel experience.

Simpler strong version

I think transport and accommodation are two of the most important things to organise before a business or study trip. Booking them early helps you avoid stress and save time. It is especially useful to stay near your workplace or study location, because this makes daily travel easier. For example, if your hotel is too far away, you may arrive late or feel tired. In conclusion, planning these things in advance makes the trip much easier and more efficient.
