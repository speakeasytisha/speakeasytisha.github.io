5Ô∏è‚É£ Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Past simple = finished time in the past (yesterday, last year, in 2019).

Present perfect = connection with now (unfinished time, experience, result now).

Compare:
I went to London last year. (finished time: last year) ‚úÖ Past simple
I have been to London three times. (experience in my life, no specific time) ‚úÖ Present perfect
