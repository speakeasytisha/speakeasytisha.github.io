Common pitfalls
  • 🇺🇸 “two thousand twenty-six” (year) is common; “twenty twenty-six” also exists, but less common for 2026.
  • Comma reading: 1,250 = “one thousand two hundred (and) fifty.”
  • For prices: “$1.50” = “one fifty” (common) or “one dollar fifty.”
