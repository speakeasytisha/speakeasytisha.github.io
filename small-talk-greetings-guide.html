    3) Grammar that makes you sound natural

    Present simple (routine) • Present continuous (now) • Present perfect (since/last time).

    Present simple (routine)

    Habits / routines: I work in customer service. I usually take the subway.

    Useful adverbs
    usually • often • sometimes • always • never
    Present continuous (now / temporary)

    Now/temporary: I’m heading to a meeting. I’m working on a project this week.

    Signals
    right now • today • this week • at the moment
    Present perfect (connection)

    Since last time: I’ve been busy lately. I’ve just started a new job.

    Signals
    lately • recently • since • for • just • already
    How are you? vs How have you been?
      If you haven’t seen them for a while → “How have you been?”
