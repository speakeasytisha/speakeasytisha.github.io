🟢 15 Seconds — Why do you want to work at Disney?

A2 Model
I would like to work at Disney because I love the Disney atmosphere and helping people. I am motivated and friendly.

B1 Model
I would really like to work at Disney because I enjoy communication, teamwork and creating magical experiences for guests. I think it would be an amazing professional opportunity for me.

🟣 30 Seconds — Describe a positive experience.

A2 Model
Last year, I worked at Carrefour Market. I helped customers and learned new vocabulary. This experience helped me become more confident speaking with people.

B1 Model
One positive experience for me was working at Carrefour Market. I learned how to communicate with customers, stay calm and work with a team. Even if I was sometimes nervous, the experience helped me become more confident and professional.

⭐ 1 Minute — Why would you be a good Disney cast member?

A2 Model
I think I would be a good Disney cast member because I am patient, motivated and friendly. I enjoy helping people and creating positive experiences. I also love learning English and improving my communication skills.

B1 Model
I believe I would be a good Disney cast member because I am motivated, patient and professional. I enjoy teamwork and communication, and I like helping people feel comfortable and happy. I am also working hard to improve my English so I can interact naturally with international guests and create magical experiences.

B1+ Upgrade Model
I believe I would be a strong Disney cast member because I am passionate about guest service, teamwork and communication. My previous experiences helped me become more confident speaking with people and solving problems calmly. I am continuing to improve my English every day because I would love to work in an international Disney environment and create memorable experiences for guests from around the world.
