🪄 Present simple: your everyday magic

When do you use it?

Use the present simple to talk about facts, routines, habits, likes, and your life in general.

Fact
I live near Nantes.
Routine

Work / Study
I study literature.
Likes

Chapter 2

👑 Pronoun magic: he, she, his, her, their

He is for a man or boy. She is for a woman or girl. They is for two or more people.

His = belongs to a man or boy. Her = belongs to a woman or girl. Their = belongs to two or more people.

My brother → he
His name is Tom.
My sister → she
Her name is Emma.
My parents → they
Their house is near Nantes.

Chapter 3

⏰ Frequency: times a week

Use once, twice, three times, four times... to say how often you do something.

once a weekune fois par semaine

I read in English once a week.

twice a weekdeux fois par semaine

I practise speaking twice a week.

four times a weekquatre fois par semaine

I go to the gym four times a week.

Number + times + a week/month/year

💖 Like, love, would like

I love + ing / noun
strong feeling

I would like + to + verb
polite dream / goal

Chapter 5

🗝️ Modal magic: can, would like, should

Modals help you sound more professional, polite, and confident. After a modal, use the base verb.

can + verb
I can help guests.
would like to + verb
I would like to improve.
should + verb
I should practise every week.
