1) Prepositions (place • time • movement)
Simple rules + vocabulary filters + easy practice.
Learn
🕒 Time
at + time (at 3:00 PM) • on + day/date (on Friday) • in + month/year (in June / in 2026)
Key words: at 10 AM, on Monday, in July
FR: à 15h → at 3 PM ; le lundi → on Monday ; en juin → in June
📍 Place
at = point/place (at the venue) • in = inside (in the ballroom) • on = surface (on the terrace)
Key words: venue, ballroom, terrace
FR: à / dans / sur (attention: “at” = lieu / point)
➡️ Movement
to (go to) • from (from the hotel) • into (into the room) • out of (out of the car)
Key words: arrive, go, walk
FR: aller à → go to ; venir de → come from
