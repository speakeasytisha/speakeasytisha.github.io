1) Key expressions you need

Les expressions importantes à connaître

📋

go through + noun

Meaning: review, check, or explain something step by step.

Traduction : revoir / passer en revue / expliquer étape par étape.

Example: We go through the safety instructions before take-off.

📞

follow up + with / on / by

Meaning: contact again or continue after a first message.

Traduction : faire un suivi / recontacter / reprendre un sujet.

Examples: I follow up with the passenger. / I follow up on the request. / I follow up by email.

🧩

What follows the verb?

After a verb, English often needs a very specific pattern.

Après un verbe, l’anglais demande souvent une structure très précise.

  • verb + noun
  • verb + preposition + noun
  • verb + to + base verb
  • verb + -ing

3) Grammar workshop — What comes after the verb?

Quelle structure vient après le verbe ?

A. Verb + noun

Some verbs go directly with a noun. There is no extra preposition.

Certains verbes vont directement avec un nom. Il n’y a pas de préposition.

✅ answer the phone

✅ check the gate number

✅ explain the procedure

❌ answer to the phone

Remember: English does not always use the same preposition as French.

B. Verb + preposition + noun

Some verbs need a fixed preposition. You must learn the whole expression together.

Certains verbes ont besoin d’une préposition fixe. Il faut apprendre l’expression complète.

Expression Translation Example
reply to répondre à I reply to emails quickly.
speak to parler à I speak to passengers politely.
ask for demander The passenger asks for help.
help with aider avec I help with cabin baggage.
prepare for se préparer à We prepare for boarding.
go through passer en revue Let’s go through the checklist.
follow up with / on / by faire un suivi I follow up with the passenger by email.

C. Verb + to + base verb

After some verbs, we use to + base verb.

Après certains verbes, on utilise to + base verb.

✅ I want to become a steward.

✅ I need to check the gate.

✅ I would like to help passengers.

❌ I want becoming a steward.

Useful verbs: want to, need to, would like to, plan to, hope to, try to.

D. Verb + -ing

After some verbs or expressions, we use verb + -ing.

Après certains verbes ou expressions, on utilise verbe + -ing.

✅ I enjoy working with people.

✅ My job consists of helping passengers.

✅ I like speaking English at work.

❌ I enjoy to work with people.

Useful verbs/expressions: enjoy, like, love, avoid, be good at, consist of.

6) Useful sentences for work

Des phrases utiles pour le travail et l’examen

Let’s go through the boarding procedure together.

Passons ensemble en revue la procédure d’embarquement.

I will follow up with you by email this afternoon.

Je ferai un suivi avec vous par email cet après-midi.

The passenger is asking for more information about the gate.

Le passager demande plus d’informations sur la porte d’embarquement.

I enjoy helping people and I want to become a steward.

J’aime aider les gens et je veux devenir steward.

7) Dialogue practice

Lis, écoute et observe les structures naturelles

Supervisor: Let’s go through the passenger list before boarding.
You: Of course. Do you want me to check the special requests too?
Supervisor: Yes, please. Then follow up with the gate agent about the family traveling to Rome.
You: No problem. I’ll follow up with the gate agent right away.

9) What to remember

go through = review/check something step by step

follow up with a person / follow up on a request / follow up by email or phone

Certains verbes ont besoin d’une préposition, d’autres de to + verbe, et d’autres de -ing.
