Grammar workshop 1 — questions with do / does

Use do and does to make questions in the present simple. Utilisez do et does pour former les questions au présent simple.

Question formula

Do/Does + subject + base verb ... ?
Do/Does + sujet + base verbale ... ?
SubjectQuestionExample
I / you / we / theyDo ... ?Do you work on weekends?
he / she / itDoes ... ?Does he answer the phone?
Important After does, the main verb goes back to the base form: Does he work? not Does he works?
Après does, le verbe principal revient à la base : Does he work? et non Does he works?

Grammar workshop 2 — negatives with don’t / doesn’t

Use do not / don’t and does not / doesn’t to say what is not true. Utilisez do not / don’t et does not / doesn’t pour dire ce qui n’est pas vrai.

Negative formula

Subject + do not / does not + base verb
Sujet + do not / does not + base verbale
SubjectNegativeExample
I / you / we / theydo not / don’tI don’t work at the airport yet.
he / she / itdoes not / doesn’tHe doesn’t use English every day.
Remember After don’t / doesn’t, use the base verb: doesn’t work, not doesn’t works.
Après don’t / doesn’t, utilisez la base verbale : doesn’t work, et non doesn’t works.
