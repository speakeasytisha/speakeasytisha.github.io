1) Start — the next step after present simple & present continuous

This lesson helps you converse by telling real stories: past simple (what happened), plus polite conversation tools.

Your mission (A2 → A2.2)

  • Tell a short story: Last week, I visited… We went… I met…
  • Ask natural questions: Did you…? What did you do? How was it?
  • React and continue: Really? That’s great! Oh no… Why?
  • Use connectors: because, so, then, after that, however
  • Speak 60–90 seconds with structure (CLOE‑friendly)

Road map (fun + friendly)

2) Grammar course — past simple for conversation (complete)

Short explanations + examples + mini checks so she knows why something is correct.

A) Past simple: affirmative

Regular verb + -ed: visit → visited, call → called

Irregular special form: go → went, meet → met, have → had

Examples:

  • I visited my son last year.
  • We went to a café.
  • I met his wife.

Mini check (QCM)

B) Past simple: negative

Rule didn’t + base verb (no -ed):

I didn’t visit • She didn’t go • We didn’t meet

Example: I didn’t travel last month because I was busy.

Mini check (QCM)

C) Questions: “Did…?”

Yes/No Did + subject + base verb?

Did you visit? • Did he call? • Did they go?

Wh‑ questions:

Where did you go? • What did you do? • How was it?

Short answers Yes, I did. / No, I didn’t.

Mini check (QCM)

D) Time markers + connectors (conversation fuel)

Time yesterday • last week • two days ago • in 2023 • when I…

Order first • then • after that • finally

Reasons because • so • that’s why

Contrast but • however

Example story: Last week I visited my son. First we had dinner, then we walked by the sea. I was happy because we had time together.

Mini check (QCM)
