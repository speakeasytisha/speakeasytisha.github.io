Writing goals

Your ideas are there. The next step is to organise them more clearly and make the message flow more naturally.

  • Start with a clear purpose.
  • Use linking expressions to guide the reader.
  • Develop each idea with a reason or example.
  • Finish with a clear conclusion or action point.

Writing formula

Purpose → Main idea → Detail/example → Clear closing

A strong formula for emails, updates, short essays, and practical professional writing.

Useful connectors

First of all, In addition, However, For example, As a result, Overall

Helpful openings

I am writing to..., In my opinion..., One important point is..., This situation shows that...

Helpful closings

For these reasons..., Overall, I believe..., Thank you for your time..., I look forward to your reply.

  • Answer directly at the beginning.
  • Add one clear reason.

Speaking formula

Answer → Reason → Example → Closing line

A reliable structure for oral exams, conversation, and spontaneous speaking.

Useful starters

In my opinion..., Personally..., I think the main reason is..., From my point of view...

To develop your answer

This is important because..., For instance..., A good example is..., Another point is that...

To finish clearly

So overall..., That is why..., For that reason..., In the end, I would say...
