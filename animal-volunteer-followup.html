1) Start

This lesson helps her present herself clearly in English: who she is, what she used to do, what she does now for animals, and why this work matters to her.

What she can say after this lesson

Language goals

  • present simple
  • past simple / used to
  • can / have to / should
  • because / so / when / before / after
  • sequencing for describing tasks

2) Lesson — grammar + useful language

Grammar 1 — Presenting yourself

Use: present simple for who you are and what you do now.

I am a former social worker. Now I volunteer in animal rescue.

FR : Je suis une ancienne travailleuse sociale. Maintenant, je fais du bénévolat dans le sauvetage animal.

Grammar 2 — Talking about the past

Use: used to or past simple for previous work.

I used to support vulnerable people and families.

FR : J’aidais des personnes et des familles vulnérables.

Grammar 3 — Ability / obligation / advice

can = ability • have to = obligation • should = advice

I can calm a nervous dog. Volunteers have to follow safety rules. We should be gentle and observant.

FR : Je peux calmer un chien nerveux. Les bénévoles doivent respecter les règles de sécurité. Nous devrions être doux et observateurs.

Grammar 4 — Explaining your routine

Sequence words: first, then, after that, finally

First, I prepare food. Then, I clean the space. After that, I check the animals. Finally, I write notes.

FR : D’abord, je prépare la nourriture. Ensuite, je nettoie l’espace. Après cela, je vérifie les animaux. Enfin, j’écris des notes.
