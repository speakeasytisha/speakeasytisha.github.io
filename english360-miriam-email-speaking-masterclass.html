1) What English 360° expects (writing + speaking)

This page is aligned to typical English 360° formats: interaction speaking (short answers), production speaking (1-minute), and writing tasks (structured texts around 125+ words). Cette page suit les formats fréquents : interaction (réponses courtes), production (1 minute), et écrits structurés (125+ mots).

The scoring “secret” (simple)
  • Structure beats complexity.
  • Politeness boosts tone and clarity.
  • Short sentences reduce errors.
  • Connectors show logic (first, then, however…).
  • La structure vaut plus que la complexité.
  • La politesse améliore le ton et la clarté.
  • Phrases courtes = moins d’erreurs.
  • Connecteurs = logique (first, then, however…).
