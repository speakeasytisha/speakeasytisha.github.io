🪄 Grammar — GOING TO vs WILL

GOING TO

Use GOING TO for plans and intentions.

I am going to improve my English.

I am going to work at Disney.

WILL

Use WILL for spontaneous decisions and predictions.

I will help you.

Your English will improve.

Choose the correct future form
