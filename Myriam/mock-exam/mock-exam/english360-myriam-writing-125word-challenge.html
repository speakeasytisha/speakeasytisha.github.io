0) Writing — how to score

Train two situations (≈125 words each): email reply / professional situation / story from images.

Winning email structure
Subject Greeting Reason (I’m writing regarding…) 2 polite requests + 1 detail Next step / confirmation Closing + name
125-word strategy
Aim for 8–12 lines. Use 3 connectors: because / however / in addition / overall. Be precise (dates, numbers, requests). Don’t write longer—write clearer.
