Record 1

Introducing yourself

Goal: say who you are, where you live, what you did professionally, and describe your personality.

🎤 Model introduction

Hello, my name is Jeff. I live in Brem-sur-Mer. I worked as an ER nurse for many years. I am calm, caring, gentle, and patient. I enjoy spending time with my family. I also love music, cycling, running, lifting weights, and playing the guitar.

Bonjour, je m’appelle Jeff. J’habite à Brem-sur-Mer. J’ai travaillé comme infirmier aux urgences pendant de nombreuses années. Je suis calme, attentionné, doux et patient. J’aime passer du temps avec ma famille. J’aime aussi la musique, faire du vélo, courir, faire de la musculation et jouer de la guitare.

🎼 Grammar focus: be + present simple

Use “be” to describe yourself

  • I am calm.
  • I am patient.
  • I am a guitarist.

Use present simple for facts and habits

  • I live in Brem-sur-Mer.
  • I enjoy music.
  • I play the guitar.
FormExampleFrench
AffirmativeI am calm.Je suis calme.
NegativeI am not impatient.Je ne suis pas impatient.
QuestionAre you patient?Es-tu patient ?
Short answerYes, I am. / No, I’m not.Oui. / Non.

Record 2

Talking about likes, hobbies, and personality

Goal: use like / love / enjoy + noun or verb-ing.

🎸 Grammar focus: like / love / enjoy

With a noun

I like blues music.

I love oldies.

With a verb

I enjoy playing the guitar.

I like biking.

Do not say

I enjoy play guitar.

I enjoy playing the guitar.

SubjectVerbExample
I / You / We / TheylikeI like music.
He / She / ItlikesJeff likes cycling.
I / You / We / TheyenjoyI enjoy playing guitar.
He / She / ItenjoysJeff enjoys running.

Record 3

Talking about music and guitar

Goal: describe music tastes and explain what music means to you.

📀 Reading: Jeff and music

Jeff loves seventies music, the blues, and the oldies. He knows many classic songs and enjoys listening carefully to great musicians. He is also an excellent guitarist and sometimes plays with groups. Music is an important part of his life because it brings feeling, memories, rhythm, and connection.

Jeff aime la musique des années soixante-dix, le blues et les anciennes chansons. Il connaît beaucoup de chansons classiques et aime écouter attentivement de grands musiciens. C’est aussi un excellent guitariste et il joue parfois avec des groupes. La musique est importante dans sa vie parce qu’elle apporte émotion, souvenirs, rythme et lien.

🎙️ Grammar focus: can / can’t

Use can to talk about ability.

FormExample
AffirmativeI can play the guitar.
NegativeI can’t sing very well.
QuestionCan you play an instrument?
Short answerYes, I can. / No, I can’t.
Important: After can, use the base verb: can play, not can plays.

Guided dialogue

A

What kind of music do you like?

I love seventies music, blues, and oldies.

Round 1 — Safe

Answer with the model sentences.

Round 2 — Personal

Change one detail: add another hobby or music style.

Round 3 — Natural

Answer without reading. Keep it simple and relaxed.
