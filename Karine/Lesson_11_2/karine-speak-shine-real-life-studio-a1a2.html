CLOE strategy

Before answering, look for the clue: usually, now, last weekend, tomorrow, I think, ever, for, or since. The clue tells you which tense to use.

15–40 min · recognise the clues

Tense Detective · Repérer les éléments de la phrase

Look at the sentence or question. First choose the tense clue. Then choose the best answer. You get immediate feedback and hints.

Present simple

usually / every / often

I go, she works.

Present continuous

now / at the moment / this week

I am going, she is waiting.

Past simple

yesterday / last / ago

I went, she worked.

Future

tomorrow / next / I think

I am meeting, I am going to, I will.

Present perfect

ever / never / for / since

I have lived, I have never been.

Confidence recap

You do not need perfect English to communicate. For CLOE, focus on clear sentences, the correct tense clue, polite phrases, and one extra detail.
