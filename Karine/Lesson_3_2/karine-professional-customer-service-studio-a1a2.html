Retail, Salon & Customer Service

This lesson helps you talk about your professional experience and handle simple customer-service situations in English. You will practise vocabulary, syntax, connectors, speaking and writing step by step.

2) Mini grammar focus: now, before, sequence

Now · present simple

I work in a salon.
I help customers.
I give advice.

Before · past simple

I worked in retail.
I helped customers.
I became a hairdresser.

Sequence

First, I worked in retail.
Then, I worked in a salon.
Finally, I want to improve my English.

Important: to advise is a verb. Advice is a noun. You can say: “I advise customers” or “I give customers advice.”

7) Final 2-minute challenge

Your final mission: Speak for 1–2 minutes using this structure.

  1. Introduce yourself.
  2. Say where you are from.
  3. Explain your past work in retail.
  4. Explain your work in a salon.
  5. Say what you like about customer contact.
  6. Finish with your English goal.

Useful start: Hello, I’m Karine. I’m from Saint-Gilles-Croix-de-Vie. First, I worked in retail in a clothing store. Then, I worked as a hairdresser in a salon. Now, I help customers and give advice. Finally, I want to improve my English.
