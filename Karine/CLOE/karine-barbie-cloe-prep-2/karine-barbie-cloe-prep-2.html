1) Mini recap · What a good CLOE answer needs

For speaking

Keep your answer simple and organised: greeting → reason → question/request → thank you.

Example: Hello. I have a reservation. Is breakfast included? Could I have a quiet room, please? Thank you.

For writing

Use short, clear lines. A good short email often includes:

Hello / Dear… → reason for writing → 2 questions → thank you → kind regards

Useful connectors

and, but, because, also, then, finally.

2) Reading comprehension

Text: You and Fabrice are planning a short stay in Sardinia.

Hello, My husband Fabrice and I are coming to Sardinia next month. We have a reservation for three nights. Could you please confirm if breakfast is included? We would also like a quiet room if possible. What time is check-out? Thank you very much, Karine
1. How long is the stay?
2. What does she ask about?

3) Listening comprehension · restaurant + paying

1. What does she order to drink?
2. How does she want to pay?

4) Guided speaking practice

Scenario: You are at the hotel reception. You want to ask about breakfast, bottled water, and check-out.

Your easy speaking plan

  1. Hello. I have a reservation.
  2. Is breakfast included?
  3. Could I have some bottled water, please?
  4. What time is check-out?
  5. Thank you very much.

5) Guided writing
