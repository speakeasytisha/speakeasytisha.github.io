1) CLOE exam focus

What you need
  • understand short everyday and professional situations
  • answer clearly with useful vocabulary and simple structure
  • write short messages, descriptions, requests and replies
  • use connectors to organise your ideas
  • show that you can manage realistic situations politely
Success strategy
  • give complete answers, not only one word
  • use a simple structure: first → details → reason → ending
  • recycle useful phrases and adapt them to the situation
  • in writing, check greeting, request, useful details and closing
  • in speaking, slow down and speak clearly

Oral mission

Scenario: You work in a salon. A customer calls to make an appointment and asks about the price of a shampoo, haircut and blow-dry. Explain the available time, give basic information, and end politely.

Model A1:
Hello. How can I help you? Yes, we have an appointment on Thursday at 2 p.m. A shampoo, haircut and blow-dry is 38 euros. Would you like to book it? Thank you. See you on Thursday.

Model A2:
Good afternoon. How can I help you? Yes, I can offer you an appointment on Thursday at 2 p.m. A shampoo, haircut and blow-dry costs 38 euros. If you would like, I can book it for you now. Could you please spell your name? Thank you very much. See you on Thursday.

Writing mission

Scenario: Write a short message to a customer to confirm an appointment and ask them to arrive 5 minutes early.

Model A1:
Hello,
Your appointment is confirmed for Friday at 10 a.m.
Please arrive 5 minutes early.
Thank you.
Kind regards,
Karine

Model A2:
Hello,
I confirm your appointment for Friday at 10 a.m. at the salon. Please arrive 5 minutes early so we can start on time. If you need to change the appointment, please let us know.
Kind regards,
Karine
