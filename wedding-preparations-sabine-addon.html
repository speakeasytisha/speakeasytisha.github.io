2) Grammar reminders

Mini lessons + common traps + hints to choose the right tense.

Present simple (routine)

Use it for habits, routines, facts: I answer emails every morning.

  • He/She/It: add -s → She calls clients.
  • Negatives: don’t / doesn’t → She doesn’t forget details.
  • Questions: Do / Does → Do you need help?
Hint words
usually • always • often • every day • on Mondays
Present continuous (now)

Use it for actions in progress / around now: I’m preparing the seating plan.

  • Form: am/is/are + -ing
  • Spelling: make → making, plan → planning
  • Temporary actions: We’re working with a new florist.
Hint words
right now • today • this week • at the moment
Future (plans + promises)

Use going to for plans: I’m going to send the schedule tomorrow.

Use will for offers / quick promises: I’ll call you back this afternoon.

Hint words
tomorrow • next week • soon • later • in two days
