Step 4

Grammar compass: past, now, future

Do not learn everything today. Just learn to recognize the direction of the sentence.

Past

I passed my exam.

When? yesterday, last week, last month, in April.

Now / habits

I learn English.

When? every week, often, sometimes, today.

Future

I am going to improve.

When? tomorrow, next week, this year.
