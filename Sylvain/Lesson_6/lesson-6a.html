01 · THE METHOD

One tense. Three forms. One question key.

For each structure, follow the same order: understand when to use it, learn the full conjugation, practise affirmative and negative sentences, then learn the exact question pattern.

Pour chaque structure, on suit le même ordre : comprendre quand l’utiliser, apprendre la conjugaison complète, pratiquer l’affirmatif et le négatif, puis apprendre la structure exacte de la question.

Core rule

Time clue + meaning + structure

A time word helps, but the meaning confirms the tense.

1 · Time clue

every day, now, at the moment, in August, tomorrow, today

2 · Meaning

routine, current action, plan, promise, schedule or state

3 · Structure

subject + verb + information → negative → question

02 · BE · identity, place and state

BE · identity, place and state

Use BE to say who someone is, where someone is, how something is, or whether something is ready.

Cette section donne la règle, la conjugaison complète, le vocabulaire utile et les exercices progressifs.

Question key

Question key: BE + subject + information? → Are you ready? / Is the order ready?

When do we use it?
  • identity: I am a chef.
  • place: We are in Savoie.
  • description/state: The order is ready.
  • temporary position: The containers are in the fridge.

Rule: BE does not use do/does. To make a question, move am/is/are before the subject.

SubjectAffirmativeNegativeQuestion
II am ready.I am not ready.Am I ready?
youYou are in Savoie.You are not in Lisbon yet.Are you in Savoie?
he / she / itShe is Portuguese.She is not French.Is she Portuguese?
weWe are a family business.We are not ready yet.Are we ready?
theyThey are oven-safe.They are not labelled.Are they labelled?

03 · HAVE / HAS · family, business, equipment

HAVE / HAS · family, business, equipment

Use HAVE to talk about family, possessions, responsibilities, equipment and order details.

Cette section donne la règle, la conjugaison complète, le vocabulaire utile et les exercices progressifs.

Question key

Question key: Do/Does + subject + have + information? → Does the aircraft have a microwave?

When do we use it?
  • family: We have two daughters.
  • business: I have a catering company.
  • equipment: The aircraft has an oven.
  • order details: The order has special requirements.

Rule: For questions and negatives, use do/does + have. After does, use have, not has.

II have a business.I do not have a restaurant now.Do I have time?
youYou have two daughters.You do not have the invoice.Do you have the invoice?
he / she / itThe aircraft has an oven.The aircraft does not have a microwave.Does the aircraft have a microwave?
weWe have a family project.We do not have a house yet.Do we have a house yet?
theyThey have dietary restrictions.They do not have fish.Do they have dietary restrictions?

04 · Present simple · routines, facts, schedules

Present simple · routines, facts, schedules

Use the present simple for facts, regular work, routines and official schedules.

Cette section donne la règle, la conjugaison complète, le vocabulaire utile et les exercices progressifs.

Question key

Question key: Do/Does + subject + base verb? → Does the flight leave? / Do you prepare orders?

When do we use it?
  • routine: We prepare meals every week.
  • fact: Airline catering is a niche market.
  • regular role: My wife works with me.
  • schedule: The flight leaves at 07:45.

Rule: For he/she/it, add -s in affirmative only. In negatives and questions, use does + base verb.

SubjectAffirmativeNegativeQuestion
II prepare orders.I do not prepare fish for this order.Do I prepare the labels?
youYou work with airlines.You do not work alone.Do you work with airlines?
he / she / itThe restaurant opens at noon.The aircraft does not have a microwave.Does the flight leave at 07:45?
weWe deliver chilled food.We do not deliver directly yet.Do we deliver directly?
theyThey need three meals.They do not need seafood.Do they need seafood?

05 · Present continuous · now, current projects, arrangements

Present continuous · now, current projects, arrangements

Use the present continuous for something happening now, a temporary situation, a current project or a fixed arrangement.

Cette section donne la règle, la conjugaison complète, le vocabulaire utile et les exercices progressifs.

Question key

Question key: Am/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing? → Are you looking? / Is the order changing?

When do we use it?
  • now: I am preparing an order now.
  • current project: We are looking for a house.
  • temporary situation: We are living in Savoie for now.
  • arrangement: I am meeting a client tomorrow.

Rule: The form is subject + am/is/are + verb-ing. Questions move am/is/are before the subject.

SubjectAffirmativeNegativeQuestion
II am preparing an order.I am not preparing fish today.Am I preparing the order?
youYou are looking for a house.You are not moving today.Are you looking for a house?
he / she / itShe is helping me.The order is not changing.Is the order changing?
weWe are moving to Lisbon.We are not moving this week.Are we moving to Lisbon?
theyThey are checking the labels.They are not waiting outside.Are they checking the labels?

06 · GOING TO · plans and intentions

GOING TO · plans and intentions

Use going to when the plan or intention already exists. It is useful for Lisbon, his business plans and future changes.

Cette section donne la règle, la conjugaison complète, le vocabulaire utile et les exercices progressifs.

Question key

Question key: Am/Is/Are + subject + going to + base verb? → Are you going to move?

When do we use it?
  • plan: We are going to move to Lisbon.
  • intention: I am going to work directly with airlines.
  • decided action: We are going to buy a house.
  • future project: We are going to develop the business.

Rule: The form is subject + am/is/are + going to + base verb. Never use verb-ing after going to.

SubjectAffirmativeNegativeQuestion
II am going to call the client.I am not going to stop working.Am I going to call the client?
youYou are going to move.You are not going to cancel.Are you going to move?
he / she / itShe is going to continue her studies.The company is not going to deliver it.Is she going to continue?
weWe are going to buy a house.We are not going to stay in Savoie.Are we going to buy a house?
theyThey are going to send the order.They are not going to change the menu.Are they going to send it?

07 · WILL · promises, decisions, predictions

WILL · promises, decisions, predictions

Use will for a promise, a decision made at the moment of speaking, an offer or a prediction.

Cette section donne la règle, la conjugaison complète, le vocabulaire utile et les exercices progressifs.

Question key

Question key: Will + subject + base verb? → Will you send the confirmation?

When do we use it?
  • promise: I will send the confirmation today.
  • decision now: I’ll check the labels now.
  • prediction: I think the delivery will be on time.
  • offer: I’ll call the client for you.

Rule: Will is easy to conjugate: same form for every subject. Questions move will before the subject.

SubjectAffirmativeNegativeQuestion
II will send the email.I will not forget the labels.Will I send the email?
youYou will receive the order.You will not need fish.Will you receive the order?
he / she / itThe delivery will be on time.It will not be late.Will it be on time?
weWe will confirm the quantity.We will not change the menu.Will we confirm the quantity?
theyThey will call tomorrow.They will not call today.Will they call tomorrow?
