02 · QUESTION MAP

The 3-step rule for questions

1Look for BE

If the verb is am / is / are, move it before the subject.

She is Portuguese. → Is she Portuguese?
2No BE? Use an auxiliary

Use do / does for present simple, will for promises/predictions, or are you going to for plans.

You prepare meals. → Do you prepare meals?
3Add a WH word if needed

Put what, where, when, why, how, which, who at the beginning.

When are you moving?
Important: after do / does / did / will / going to, use the base verb: Does the aircraft have…? not has.
03 · BE QUESTIONS

Am / is / are questions

Use this family for identity, place, description, state and present continuous.

On utilise cette famille pour l’identité, le lieu, la description, l’état et le présent continu.

Formula

Am / Is / Are + subject + information?

Are you ready? · Is your wife Portuguese? · Are you moving?

04 · DO / DOES QUESTIONS

Present simple questions: routines, facts and regular work

Use do with I/you/we/they. Use does with he/she/it. After do/does, the verb stays in the base form.

On utilise do avec I/you/we/they et does avec he/she/it. Après do/does, le verbe reste à la base verbale.

Formula

Do / Does + subject + base verb?

Do you work with airlines? · Does the order include fruit?
✅ Correct

Does the aircraft have a microwave?

Does your wife work with you?

❌ Common mistake

Does the aircraft has a microwave?

Does your wife works with you?

05 · FUTURE QUESTIONS

Plans, promises, arrangements and schedules

Future time words do not choose the tense alone. First look at the time clue. Then decide the meaning.

Les mots de temps au futur ne choisissent pas le temps tout seuls. Cherchez d’abord l’indice de temps, puis le sens.

🔑

Time clue + meaning = tense
Tomorrow can be a plan, arrangement, promise or schedule.

Plan / intention

Are you going to move to Lisbon?

going to
Fixed arrangement

Are you meeting the client tomorrow?

present continuous
Promise / prediction

Will you send the confirmation today?

will
Official schedule

When does the flight leave?

present simple

08 · PROFESSIONAL QUESTIONS

Airline catering, orders, labels and aircraft equipment

These questions help you check important details: order, delivery time, flight, dietary restrictions, packaging and aircraft equipment.

Ces questions permettent de vérifier les détails essentiels : commande, heure de livraison, vol, restrictions alimentaires, emballage et équipement de l’avion.

📦 Order details

  • Which flight is this order for?
  • How many meals do you need?
  • What time is the delivery?
  • Does the order include special meals?
  • Who should we label the meals for?

🏷️ Labels & dietary needs

  • Are there any allergies?
  • Are there any dietary restrictions?
  • Should we label each item in English?
  • Should the sauce be packed separately?
  • Do you need reheating instructions?

✈️ Aircraft & packaging

  • Does the aircraft have a microwave?
  • Does the aircraft have an oven?
  • Do the containers need to be oven-safe?
  • What is the maximum container size?
  • Should we use half-atlas trays?
Professional examples are based on his order and equipment context: delivery to aircraft, dietary restrictions, English labels, limited onboard space and oven/microwave requirements.
