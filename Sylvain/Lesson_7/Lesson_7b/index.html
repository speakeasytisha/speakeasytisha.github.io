02 · THE PAST SIMPLE MAP

The essential structure

✅ AFFIRMATIVE Subject + past verb + information

I started my business.

The company contacted me.

🚫 NEGATIVE Subject + didn’t + base verb

I didn’t start alone.

They didn’t contact my friend again.

❓ QUESTION Did + subject + base verb?

Did the company contact you?

When did you start?

Important: after did or didn’t, use the base verb.
✅ Did they contact you? · I didn’t start immediately.
❌ Did they contacted you? · I didn’t started immediately.
03 · PAST TIME CLUES

Past clues usually show a finished action.

Use the past simple when the action is finished and the time is finished or clearly in the past.

Utilisez le prétérit quand l’action est terminée et que le moment est terminé ou clairement dans le passé.

Signal words

yesterday · last week · last month · last year · two years ago · in 2018 · when I was younger · before · at the beginning · after that · later · finally

Personal

Last year, we visited Portugal.

When I was younger, I learned English at school.

Professional

A company contacted me because they needed a catering service.

First, we discussed the request. Then, we prepared the meals.

05 · ESSENTIAL IRREGULAR VERBS

Some verbs do not use -ed.

These are the first irregular verbs Sylvain needs for personal and professional storytelling.

Certains verbes ne prennent pas -ed. Ce sont les verbes irréguliers essentiels pour raconter son parcours personnel et professionnel.

Strategy

Learn irregulars in useful sentences, not as a long isolated list.

be → was / were

I was a chef. They were clients.

have → had

We had an opportunity.

go → went

I went to Geneva.

make → made

We made meals for a flight.

meet → met

I met an airline contact.

find → found

We found a solution.

begin → began

The project began unexpectedly.

become → became

It became a regular activity.

send → sent

They sent us the order.

speak → spoke

I spoke to the client.

come → came

The request came from a company.

do → did

What did you do?

07 · NEGATIVE SENTENCES

Use didn’t + base verb.

Subject + didn’t + base verb + information

I didn’t start immediately. · We didn’t deliver the order late. · They didn’t contact my friend again.

Key rule: after didn’t, use the base verb.
✅ I didn’t start. · We didn’t prepare it yesterday.
❌ I didn’t started. · We didn’t prepared it yesterday.

08 · PAST QUESTIONS

Questions are the priority: did, was, were.

❓ Action verbs → DID

Did + subject + base verb?

Did the company contact you?

When did you start?

Pour les verbes d’action au passé, utilisez did, puis le verbe de base.

❓ BE in the past → WAS / WERE

Was / Were + subject + information?

Was the order ready?

Were the clients satisfied?

Avec le verbe être au passé, utilisez was / were, pas did.

Quick decision

  1. If the sentence has was / were, move it before the subject: Was it ready?
  2. If the sentence has an action verb, use did: Did they contact you?
  3. After did, always use the base verb: Did they contact? not contacted.

09 · OBJECT PRONOUNS

Who did what to whom?

Object pronouns help you complete the sentence naturally: The company contacted me. They sent us the order. We worked with them.

Les pronoms compléments permettent de compléter naturellement la phrase : qui a fait l’action à qui ?

Position

Use object pronouns after a verb or after a preposition: contacted me, sent us, worked with them.

SubjectObjectExample
ImeThe company contacted me.
youyouI called you.
hehimWe met him at the airport.
sheherI spoke to her yesterday.
ititWe delivered it this morning.
weusThey sent us the order.
theythemWe prepared meals for them.
Careful: her can be an object pronoun or a possessive adjective.
Object: I called her. · Possessive: Her daughter is 12.
