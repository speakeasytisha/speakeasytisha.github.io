02 · PAST · WHERE YOUR STORY BEGAN

Use the past simple for completed chapters.

Use the past simple to explain training, former jobs, important decisions and achievements with a finished time reference.

Utilisez le prétérit pour expliquer la formation, les anciens postes, les décisions importantes et les réussites liées à un moment terminé.

Past-simple backbone

Subject + past verb + detail

I started my career near Lake Aiguebelette.
At 18, I joined a prestigious brigade.

✓

Affirmative

I started.
I joined.
I worked.
I returned.

Use the past form of the verb.
×

Negative

I did not start.
I didn’t join.
I didn’t work.

After didn’t, use the base verb.
?

Question

Did you start?
Did you join?
Where did you work?

After did, use the base verb.
PAST PRACTICE

Choose the strongest career sentence.

Look for a completed time reference. Use the past simple, not the present form.

03 · PRESENT · WHAT YOU DO NOW

Present your business world with precision.

Use the present simple for your current role, regular services, facts and business identity. Use the present continuous only for a project happening around now.

Utilisez le présent simple pour votre rôle actuel, les services habituels, les faits et l’identité de vos entreprises. Utilisez le présent continu uniquement pour un projet en cours.

Now: simple or continuous?

We run three complementary activities. (fact / regular role)

We are preparing a new project. (current project)

RESTAURANT LE BISTROT

Fine, sincere cuisine in Chambéry.

Speak about seasonal products, the historic setting, the restaurant’s style and its recognition.

  • We welcome guests in Chambéry.
  • We create seasonal menus with fresh products.
  • Our cuisine is refined, sincere and product-led.

RESTAURANT L’ÉPHÉMÈRE

A seasonal restaurant at Le Bourget-du-Lac.

Use it to describe a second, seasonal dining experience and a warm, accessible style.

  • We operate a seasonal restaurant from May to September.
  • We offer generous, accessible food.
  • Guests can enjoy the terrace and a welcoming atmosphere.

ENJOY · INFLIGHT CATERING

Original menus designed for travel.

Use it to explain airline catering, quality, delivery and the passenger experience.

  • We create inspiring menus for airline clients.
  • We focus on quality, presentation and reliable delivery.
  • We provide inflight catering and concierge services.
PRESENT PRACTICE

Present simple or present continuous?

Ask: Is this a regular role/fact, or is it a current temporary project?

04 · FUTURE · THE NEXT CHAPTER

Talk about Lisbon and future business plans with the right meaning.

The future is not one tense. Choose the form that matches the message: plan, arrangement, promise or timetable.

Le futur ne correspond pas à un seul temps. Choisissez la forme selon le sens : projet, rendez-vous organisé, promesse ou horaire officiel.

🇵🇹

Remember:
Time words such as in August tell you when. Meaning tells you which future form to use.

01

Plan / intention

be going to + base verb

We are going to move to Lisbon in August.

Key ideas: plan · intend · decided · goal
02

Arrangement

be + verb-ing

We are meeting airline clients next week.

Key ideas: booked · arranged · appointment · meeting
03

Promise / decision

will + base verb

I will send the proposal this afternoon.

Key ideas: I promise · don’t worry · I think · probably
04

Schedule

present simple

The flight leaves at 07:45.

Key ideas: timetable · schedule · flight · starts · arrives
✦
Your next chapter: In Lisbon, I am going to build on my experience in restaurants and airline catering. I want to work more directly with airline clients and continue to create food that is memorable, reliable and beautifully presented.
