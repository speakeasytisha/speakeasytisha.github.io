Next steps

How to build on these results

Your report already gives useful suggestions. Here is a clear lesson version of them.

For listening

Keep listening to longer talks, podcasts, debates, and documentaries. Focus on opinions, attitude, stress, and emphasis.

For reading

Read longer professional texts, compare articles, and practise short summaries so you keep using your strong reading skill actively.

For writing

Write more opinion texts, reviews, reports, and short essays. Keep giving reasons, examples, and clearer evaluations.

For speaking

Practise fluency, pronunciation, collocations, and giving opinions aloud. The goal is to make your ideas come out more smoothly and confidently.
