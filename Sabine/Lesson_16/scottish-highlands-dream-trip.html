Why the Scottish Highlands?

The Highlands are perfect for a fun English lesson: mountains, lochs, castles, trains, hotels, villages, viewpoints, tickets, timetables, weather, food and dream itineraries. You can use simple English naturally: asking questions, describing places, choosing options, giving opinions and planning a trip.

Les Highlands sont parfaits pour une leçon d’anglais vivante : paysages, châteaux, hôtels, trains, billets, itinéraires et questions pratiques.

Glencoe

🏔️ valley = valléea low area between mountainsExample: Glencoe is a beautiful valley.
⛰️ mountain = montagnea very high natural hillExample: The mountains are impressive.
🛣️ road = routea way for cars and busesExample: The road goes through Glencoe.
👀 view = vuewhat you can seeExample: The view is amazing.

Try: “I like Glencoe. It looks beautiful.”

I like Glencoe. It is a famous valley in the Highlands. The mountains are beautiful and the view is amazing.

Glenfinnan Viaduct

🚂 steam train = train à vapeuran old-style train with steamExample: I want to take the steam train.
🌉 viaduct = viaduca long bridge for trainsExample: The train goes over the viaduct.
🎫 ticket = billeta pass for travelExample: I need a train ticket.
🪟 window view = vue par la fenêtrethe scenery you see from the trainExample: The window view is fantastic.

Try: “I want to take the train. The view looks amazing.”

I want to visit Glenfinnan. I want to take the steam train. The viaduct is famous and the view looks amazing.

Loch Ness & Urquhart Castle

Part 7 · Phone, speaking & writing

Use the same trip theme to practise real communication with easier support.

Phone call · asking about a hotel

Task: call a hotel and ask for information.

Hello, this is Sabine calling.

I would like to book a room.

Is breakfast included?

Is the hotel near the station?

Thank you for your help.

Receptionist: Good afternoon, Glen View Hotel. How can I help you?
You: Hello, this is Sabine calling. I would like to book a room.
Receptionist: Of course. A single room or a double room?
You: A double room, please. Is breakfast included?
Receptionist: Yes, it is.
You: Great. Is the hotel near the station?
Receptionist: Yes, it is about ten minutes away by taxi.
You: Perfect. Thank you for your help.
