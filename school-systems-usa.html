5) Language support for foreign students (ELL / ML learners)

Public schools must take action to help English learners access education. Districts decide the exact program model, but core civil-rights protections apply.

What‚Äôs generally required (federal)
  • Identify English learners and provide an effective language program.
  • Communicate meaningfully with families (interpretation/translation as needed).
  • Avoid discrimination based on national origin / language barriers.
