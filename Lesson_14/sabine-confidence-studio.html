Part 4 · 16 minutes

Tense toolkit: one tense at a time

Stay in one tense before you move to the next one. First read the time marker and the French clue. Then practise the form.

Step 1 · Choose the tense

Read the time marker. Then choose the best tense.

Tense block 1

🌿 Present simple

Use: routine, job, usual tasks

Keywords: usually, often, every week, on Mondays
d’habitude, souvent, chaque semaine, le lundi

Affirmative: I organise weddings.

Negative: I do not / don't organise weddings.

Question: Do I organise weddings?

Step by step

  1. Start with the subject: I
  2. Add the base verb: organise
  3. For the negative, add do not / don't + base verb
  4. For the question, start with Do + subject + base verb

Practice · Present simple only

Stay with this tense only: first I, then a mixed pronoun.

Tense block 2

📍 Present continuous

Use: now, in progress, current action

Keywords: now, right now, today, at the moment
maintenant, en ce moment, aujourd’hui, actuellement

Affirmative: I am organising the event.

Negative: I am not organising the event.

Question: Am I organising the event?

Step by step

  1. Start with the subject: I
  2. Add am
  3. Add the verb + -ing: organising
  4. For the question, move am to the front

Practice · Present continuous only

Stay with this tense only: first I, then a mixed pronoun.

Tense block 3

➡️ Be going to

Use: next step, planned future

Keywords: tomorrow, next week, soon
demain, la semaine prochaine, bientôt

Affirmative: I am going to contact the florist.

Negative: I am not going to contact the florist.

Question: Am I going to contact the florist?

Step by step

  1. Start with the subject: I
  2. Add am
  3. Add going to
  4. Add the base verb: contact

Practice · Be going to only

Stay with this tense only: first I, then a mixed pronoun.

Tense block 4

🕰️ Past simple

Use: finished action in the past

Keywords: yesterday, last week, last month, in Paris
hier, la semaine dernière, le mois dernier, à Paris

Affirmative: I organised the salon.

Negative: I did not / didn't organise the salon.

Question: Did I organise the salon?

Step by step

  1. Start with the subject: I
  2. In the affirmative, use the past form: organised
  3. In the negative, use did not / didn't + base verb
  4. In the question, use Did + subject + base verb

Practice · Past simple only

Stay with this tense only: first I, then a mixed pronoun.

Part 7 · 5 minutes

CLOE mini practice

🎤 Speaking card

You organised a salon in Paris last week. Explain what you did before the event, during the event, and after the event.

Safe frame: First, I… Then, I… During the salon, I… After that, I… Finally, I…

Last week, I organised a salon in Paris. First, I contacted the suppliers and I prepared the schedule. Then, I checked the guest list and the welcome desk. During the salon, I answered questions, I greeted visitors, and I coordinated the team. After that, I spoke with new contacts and I noted useful information. Finally, I reviewed the event and I prepared the next follow-up email.

La semaine dernière, j’ai organisé un salon à Paris. D’abord, j’ai contacté les prestataires et j’ai préparé le planning. Ensuite, j’ai vérifié la liste des invités et l’accueil. Pendant le salon, j’ai répondu aux questions, j’ai accueilli les visiteurs et j’ai coordonné l’équipe. Après cela, j’ai parlé avec de nouveaux contacts et j’ai noté des informations utiles. Enfin, j’ai relu l’événement et j’ai préparé le prochain e-mail de suivi.

✍️ Writing card

Write a short follow-up email after a venue meeting.

Safe route: greeting → thank you → summary → question / next step → closing

Subject: Thank you for today’s venue meeting

Dear Madam,
Thank you for your time today. It was a pleasure to meet you and review the venue details.
We confirmed the ceremony space, the welcome area, and the timetable for the day.
Could you please send me the final room capacity by Friday?
Thank you very much for your help.
Best regards,
Sabine

Objet : Merci pour la réunion d’aujourd’hui au lieu
Bonjour Madame,
Merci pour votre temps aujourd’hui. C’était un plaisir de vous rencontrer et de revoir les détails du lieu.
Nous avons confirmé l’espace cérémonie, la zone d’accueil et le planning de la journée.
Pourriez-vous m’envoyer la capacité finale des salles avant vendredi ?
Merci beaucoup pour votre aide.
Cordialement,
Sabine
