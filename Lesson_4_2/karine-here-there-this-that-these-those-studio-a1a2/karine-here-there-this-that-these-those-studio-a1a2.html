🏄‍♀️ Here, There, This, That, These & Those

In this lesson, you will practise how to point to people, objects, and places clearly. You will learn how to use here / there and this / that / these / those in salon, retail, and customer-service situations. You will also build better syntax in simple sentences and short messages.

Goal

By the end of the lesson, you should be able to say where something is, choose the correct demonstrative, and write short, clear client-facing messages.

Mini tip for French speakers

English often needs you to show distance (near / far) and number (singular / plural). Ask yourself two questions:
1) Is it near me or far from me?
2) Is it one thing or several things?
