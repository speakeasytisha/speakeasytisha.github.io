🌊 Small Words After Verbs

In this lesson, you will learn how small words such as back, in, out, up, down, on, off, through, around, and with change the meaning of a verb. This is a key difference between English and French, and understanding the logic will make your English clearer and more natural.

1) Logic first · what do these small words do?

French guide · Guide en français

In English, the small word after the verb often changes the meaning.
En anglais, le petit mot après le verbe change souvent le sens. Think of the particle as a direction or an extra idea: return, inside, outside, up, down, together, etc.

back

Return / again / to the original place
FR : retour / de nouveau / au point de départ
come back, call back

in

Inside / enter / include
FR : à l’intérieur / entrer / inclure
come in, fill in

out

Outside / remove / leave
FR : dehors / sortir / retirer
go out, take out

up

Higher / complete / ready
FR : vers le haut / complètement / prêt
look up, clean up

down

Lower / on paper / calmer
FR : vers le bas / sur papier / plus calme
sit down, write down

on / off

On body / active vs remove / inactive
FR : sur soi / actif ou allumé vs enlever / éteint ou inactif
try on, turn off

through

From start to finish / across
FR : de bout en bout / à travers
go through, talk through

around

In different places / in the area
FR : dans différents endroits / dans les environs
look around, show around

with

Together / using
FR : ensemble / avec / au moyen de
work with, pay with
