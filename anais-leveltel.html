Transcript of the phone call

Anaïs: Good morning, Anaïs speaking.
Peter: Hello Anaïs, this is Peter Kovács from HungarTech. I'm calling about our follow-up meeting scheduled for next Tuesday at three p.m.
Anaïs: Yes, hello Peter. Nice to hear from you. Is there any problem with the meeting?
Peter: Not really a problem, but there is a small issue with the time. Three p.m. French time is very late for my team in Hungary. Could we move it to earlier in the day?
Anaïs: I see. Let me check… Would ten a.m. French time work for you?
Peter: Ten a.m. French time is perfect, that’s eleven in Budapest. Everyone will still be in the office.
Anaïs: Great. So we move the meeting to next Tuesday at ten a.m. French time. I’ll send you a confirmation email with the updated calendar invite.
Peter: Thank you. Could you also add our new project manager, Zsófia Tóth, to the invitation?
Anaïs: Of course. Could you spell her email address for me, please?
Peter: Yes, it’s zsofia.toth@hungartech.hu.
Anaïs: Perfect, I’ve got it. You will receive the confirmation in a few minutes.
Peter: Thanks a lot, Anaïs. Have a nice day!
Anaïs: You too, Peter. Bye-bye.
