Safe speaking frame

The 6-sentence past story

1. When? Last weekend, I went to the mountains.

2. Who with? I was with my husband and my dog.

3. Activities? We walked and relaxed.

4. Food? We had lunch at home.

5. Opinion? It was quiet and pleasant.

6. Reason? I enjoyed it because I like nature.

Step 3

Grammar focus: Past Simple

Frankie does not need complicated grammar. She needs a safe pattern she can use again and again.

✅

Positive sentences

Subject + past verb

I visited my family. · We walked. · I went shopping.

➖

Negative sentences

didn’t + base verb

I didn’t work. · We didn’t go out. · She didn’t eat at the restaurant.

❓

Questions

Did + subject + base verb?

Did you go? · Did she work? · What did you do?

🌤️

Was / were

Use for feelings, weather and places

It was nice. · We were happy. · The hotel was comfortable.

Regular verbs

walk → walked

visit → visited

relax → relaxed

Irregular verbs

go → went

have → had

eat → ate

Very important

✅ Did you go?

❌ Did you went?

After did, the verb is simple: go, visit, eat, work.

Step 8

Speaking practice: tell a past story

Use the cards below to make a complete answer. Frankie can choose the A2 version first, then try the A2+ version.

A2 safe model

Last weekend, I stayed at home. I cooked, watched TV and relaxed. It was quiet. I enjoyed my weekend because I needed to rest.

A2+ target model

Last weekend, I went for a walk with my husband and my dog. We walked in the countryside and had lunch at home. The weather was nice, so it was a good day.

B1- stretch model

Last weekend was simple but very pleasant. I spent time with my husband and my dog. We went for a walk, enjoyed the fresh air and relaxed at home afterwards.
