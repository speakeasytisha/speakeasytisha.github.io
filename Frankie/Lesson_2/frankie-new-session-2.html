Step 3

Grammar focus: Present Simple for routine

Use the present simple to talk about habits, routines, facts and things that happen regularly.

Today: focus on “I”

I + usually/often/sometimes + verb + time

I usually start work at 8.

I sometimes go shopping after work.

Be careful with he / she

I startwork at 8.
She startswork at 8.
I watchTV.
He watchesTV.

Note: talking about yourself.

Questions with “do”

Do + you + verb?

Do you work every day?

What time do you start work?

Practice 1 · Choose the correct verb

Select the best answer. Then check your score.

Hint: with I / you / we / they, use the simple verb. With he / she / it, add -s or -es.

Step 4

Time expressions + frequency

These small words make your answers clearer and more natural.

AT

Use at for precise times.

at 8 o’clock · at 4 p.m. · at lunchtime

ON

Use on for days and dates.

on Monday · on Saturday · on the weekend

IN

Use in for parts of the day, months and seasons.

in the morning · in May · in summer

Practice 2 · at / on / in

Hint: exact time = at, day = on, part of the day = in.

Practice 3 · frequency position

Choose the natural sentence.

Hint: frequency adverbs usually come before the main verb: I usually start, I often go, I sometimes watch.

Step 5

Word order practice

You need English syntax to feel predictable. Use this pattern: subject + frequency + verb + details + time.

Subject Frequency Verb Object / place Time

Practice 4 · Put the words in order

Type the full sentence. Capital letters are not important, but punctuation is good practice.

1. usually / I / breakfast / have / at 7

2. work / I / start / at 8

3. after work / I / sometimes / watch TV

4. at the weekend / I / like / spending time / with my family

5. because / I / English / learn / want to travel

Step 6

Question builder: speak and interact

Practises asking and answering simple questions. This helps you avoid freezing in real conversations.

Choose a question type

Answer upgrade

Move from a short answer to a stronger A2 answer.

Question: What do you usually do after work?

Short answer: I relax.

Better answer: After work, I usually relax at home. Sometimes I watch TV or go for a walk.

Practice 5 · Match the question and the answer

Hint: listen for the question word: what time = hour, what do you do = activity, why = because.

Step 7

Listening: a normal but busy week

Listen first without reading. Then answer the questions. After that, reveal the transcript and listen again.

Transcript

My week is quite simple, but sometimes it is busy. I usually start work at eight or nine and I finish at about four in the afternoon. After work, I often relax at home. Sometimes I go shopping or go for a walk with my dog. At the weekend, I like spending time with my family because it is important for me.
