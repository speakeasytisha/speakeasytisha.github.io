Part 1

Getting to know you — magical interview

Choose your answers in the dropdown menus. You can also add your own details. Then build your introduction.

Question:
How are you today?
Expected answer:
I’m feeling happy / nervous / motivated today.
Question:
Why do you need English?
Expected answer:
I need English for work, travel and the VTest.
Question:
What is your dream job?
Expected answer:
I would like to work in a magical theme park.
Correction focus:
to be, present simple, word order, he/she, at/on/in.

Part 2

Answer upgrade lab — make your sentences stronger

You already answered the questions in Part 1. Now improve your English: choose a simple idea, then upgrade it with a reason, a connector, or a useful detail.

Part 3

Dialogue builder — first conversation

Practise a first meeting conversation. Choose the best answer, listen, then repeat.

Part 4

Role play builder — magical park guest service

Imagine you work in a magical theme park. A visitor needs help. Choose your phrases and practise the conversation with your teacher.

Part 5

Mini VTest practice — simple but strategic
