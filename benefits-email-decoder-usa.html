2026 Benefits Summary

Benefit eligibility starts on the 1st of the month following 30 days of full‑time employment.

Health plan option A: Surest — choose an in‑network provider and see what you will pay for each service before care. Company pays 100% of employee premium. Spouse/children/family can be added with an extra cost.

Health plan option B: UHC Choice 2500 — major medical + wellness + prescription benefits. Company pays 100% of employee premium. Dependents cost extra.

Health plan option C: UHC Choice 1000 — major medical + wellness + prescriptions. Company pays 80% of employee premium. Employee pays the rest + dependents cost extra.

Dental — employer pays 50% of premium. Plans may include an annual maximum benefit.

Vision — employer pays 50% of premium.

Section 125 FSA — set aside pre‑tax money for medical reimbursement (limit applies).

Short‑Term Disability (STD) — supplemental pay (example: up to 60% of base earnings for up to 26 weeks). Automatically enrolled.

Long‑Term Disability (LTD) — supplemental pay after a longer waiting time (example: after 180 days). Automatically enrolled.

Life insurance — employer paid, fixed death benefit.

401(k) — retirement savings with an employer match after eligibility.

PTO — paid time off (holidays, vacation, sick time). Some policies allow rollover.
