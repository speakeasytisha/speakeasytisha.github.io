GET + (particle) = new meaning

This lesson teaches phrasal verbs where a small change (up / over / by / along / through…) can completely change meaning. It is structured and gradual: learn → practice by category → mixed review → role-play.

Lesson time
75–90 min
Targets

Outcome

Session plan (teacher-friendly)
0–15 min: Clear rules + “GET map” + separable vs inseparable. 15–30 min: Step 1 — Core GET verbs (daily life + work + travel). 30–45 min: Step 2 — Object placement (separable vs inseparable). 45–60 min: Step 3 — Advanced GET (rid of, through, away with, into…). 60–75 min: Step 4 — Mixed review (exam speed). 75–90 min: Role-play speaking + writing (multiple scenarios + models).
