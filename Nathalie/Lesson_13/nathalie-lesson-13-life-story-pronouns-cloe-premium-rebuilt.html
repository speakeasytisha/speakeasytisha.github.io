Useful starters

My name is...I grew up...Later, I...After that...Nowadays...I am proud of myself because...In the future...
My name is Nathalie. I grew up near Nantes. I studied in Paris. I worked with clients. Nowadays, I am retired, and I enjoy travelling and learning English.
My name is Nathalie, and I live near Nantes in the west of France. I grew up in a pleasant area, and later I studied in Paris. After that, I worked with clients for many years and developed strong communication skills. My children and I are very close, and I enjoy spending time with my family. Nowadays, I am retired, so I have more time for travelling, African dance and English. I am proud of myself because I did not give up. In the future, I am going to travel more and continue discovering new cultures.
