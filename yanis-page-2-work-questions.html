Grammar flight deck

On this page, you need to control questions and negatives in the present simple.

1. Questions with do

Use do with I / you / we / they.

QuestionDo you work on weekends?
Short answerYes, I do. / No, I don’t.
PatternDo + subject + base verb?

2. Negatives with don’t

Use don’t with I / you / we / they.

AffirmativeI answer emails.
NegativeI don’t answer every email in English.
PatternSubject + don’t + base verb

3. WH- questions

Use a question word + do + subject + base verb.

WhatWhat do you do?
WhereWhere do you work?
WhenWhen do you use English?
WhyWhy do you want to become a steward?

4. Important reminder

After do / don’t, use the base verb.

Do you work...? I don’t work... not: Do you works...? not: I don’t working...

Your writing / speaking space

Model answer:

At the moment, I work as a receptionist in a three-star hotel. I work at reception and I welcome guests, answer phone calls, reply to emails, and deal with reservations. I use English mainly on the phone, by email, and with international guests. In the future, I want to become a steward because I enjoy customer contact, service, and international communication.
