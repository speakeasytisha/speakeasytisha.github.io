03 · Grammar engine

The present simple

Le présent simple

I work. You work. He works.

When do we use it?

Use the present simple for routines, facts, jobs, likes and regular activities.

On utilise le présent simple pour les habitudes, les faits, le travail, les goûts et les activités régulières.

routinejobfamily factslikes

✅ Affirmative

I / You / We / They + verb

I work in a gym.

He / She / It + verb + -s

She works in a gym.

🚫 Negative

I / You / We / They + do not / don’t + verb

I don’t work on Sundays.

He / She / It + does not / doesn’t + verb

She doesn’t work on Sundays.

❓ Question

Do + I / you / we / they + verb?

Do you work in a gym?

Does + he / she / it + verb?

Does she work in a gym?

⚠️
Important: After does / doesn’t, use the basic verb: Does she work? — not Does she works?

Après does / doesn’t, on utilise le verbe à la base : Does she work ? — pas Does she works ?

He / She / It: what changes?

Third-person spelling rules

Les règles d’orthographe pour he / she / it.

Most verbswork → worksShe works.
-s, -sh, -ch, -x, -ogo → goesHe goes.
consonant + ystudy → studiesShe studies.
havehave → hasHe has a job.

04 · Time & place

Prepositions: at, on and in

Les prépositions : at, on et in

at · on · in

One small word can change the meaning.

Use the simple clues below. You do not need to learn everything today — just begin with the most useful patterns.

Un petit mot peut changer le sens. Vous n’avez pas besoin de tout apprendre aujourd’hui : commencez avec les modèles les plus utiles.

AT

specific point

point précis

  • at 7 a.m.
  • at work
  • at reception
  • at the gym

I start work at 8 a.m.

ON

day / surface

jour / surface

  • on Monday
  • on Sunday
  • on the timetable
  • on the phone

I work on Monday.

IN

city / country / part of day

ville / pays / moment de la journée

  • in Paris
  • in the UK
  • in the morning
  • in a fitness centre

I want to work in the UK.
