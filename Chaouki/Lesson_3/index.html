03 · Grammar focus

The present continuous

Le présent continu

am / is / are + verb-ing

When do we use it?

Use the present continuous for an action happening now, around now, or for a temporary current project.

On utilise le présent continu pour une action qui se passe maintenant, autour de maintenant, ou pour un projet temporaire en cours.

nowtodayat the momentcurrentlythis week

I am reading a job advert now. Je lis une annonce d’emploi maintenant.

We are planning our move this year. Nous préparons notre déménagement cette année.

✅ Affirmative

Subject + am / is / are + verb-ing

  • I am looking for a job.
  • My wife is reading school information.
  • We are comparing schools.

🚫 Negative

Subject + am not / isn’t / aren’t + verb-ing

  • I am not working in the UK yet.
  • She isn’t applying today.
  • We aren’t moving this month.

❓ Questions

Am / Is / Are + subject + verb-ing?

  • Are you looking for work?
  • Is she reading the advert?
  • Are they visiting the school?

1. Most verbs: add -ing

work → working
look → looking
read → reading

2. Verb ending in -e: remove -e

live → living
manage → managing
write → writing

3. Short vowel + consonant: double the final consonant

run → running
plan → planning
sit → sitting

4. British spelling note

travel → travelling
cancel → cancelling
En anglais britannique, on double souvent le “l”.

⚠️
Be careful: Do not say I looking for a job. You need am / is / are: I am looking for a job.

Attention : il faut toujours am / is / are avec le verbe en -ing.

04 · Grammar comparison

Present simple or present continuous?

Présent simple ou présent continu ?

routine ↔ current project

Present simple

Use it for routine, facts, permanent situations and regular activities.

Pour les habitudes, les faits, les situations stables et les activités régulières.

usuallyevery dayalwayson Mondays
  • I manage a gym in France.
  • My daughter goes to school.
  • We live in Paris.

Present continuous

Use it for now, around now, or a temporary project.

Pour maintenant, autour de maintenant ou un projet temporaire en cours.

nowtodayat the momentcurrently
  • I am looking for work in the UK.
  • We are visiting schools this week.
  • My wife is completing a form.
One person, two truths: I manage a gym. (usual job) I am looking for a job in the UK. (current project)

Une même personne peut avoir une habitude et un projet actuel.

05 · Grammar for a new area

There is / There are + some / any

There is / There are + some / any

a place · places · questions

Use it to describe what exists in a place

Use there is for one thing and there are for two or more things.

Utilisez there is pour une chose et there are pour deux choses ou plus.

✅ Positive

There is a primary school near the park.

There are two fitness centres in the town centre.

🚫 Negative

There isn’t a nursery near our flat.

There aren’t any school places available.

❓ Questions

Is there a bus stop nearby?

Are there any changing rooms?

some — usually positive sentences

There are some good schools in this area.

any — usually questions and negatives

Are there any school places?
There aren’t any places today.

📍
Useful place phrases: in the town centre · near the park · next to the station · opposite the school · at reception.

Phrases utiles : dans le centre-ville · près du parc · à côté de la gare · en face de l’école · à l’accueil.
