Quick reminder

Present simple = routine / fact

I manage two clubs.
We review the figures every week.

Present continuous = now / current action

I am meeting a manager today.
We are reviewing the figures now.

Présent simple : habitude / fait. Présent continu : action en cours ou prévue maintenant.

03 · GRAMMAR FOR YOUR MEETING

How many? Some, any & countable words

A. Countable and uncountable nouns

Countable nouns can be counted: one member, two members; one cancellation, three cancellations.

Uncountable nouns are not normally counted one by one: staff, equipment, information, feedback, work.

✓ There are 11 members of staff. → 11 salariés
✓ There is some equipment to repair. → du matériel à réparer
✗ There are 11 staffs. → staff ne prend pas de -s ici.

B. How many? + plural noun

Use How many…? for people and things you can count.

How many + plural noun + are there?

How many new members are there?
There are 18 new members.

How many cancellations are there?
There are 3 cancellations.

Combien de… y a-t-il ? Utilisez un nom pluriel après How many.

C. Some / any / a lot of

some = an affirmative quantity

There are some new sign-ups.

any = questions and negative sentences

Are there any cancellations?
There aren’t any complaints.

a lot of = a large quantity

We have a lot of work this week.

Some dans une phrase affirmative ; any dans les questions et les phrases négatives.

Meeting strategy: You do not need long sentences. Say one fact, one issue, then one action. Vous n’avez pas besoin de longues phrases : un fait, un problème, puis une action.

05 · MODEL DIALOGUES & ROLE-PLAYS

Your Monday management meeting

🤝

1. Start the meeting

Your aim: greet the manager and begin the review.

Votre objectif : saluer le manager et commencer le point.

You: Good morning. Thank you for meeting me today.

Manager: Good morning. You’re welcome.

You: Let’s review the figures for your club.

Vous : Bonjour. Merci de me rencontrer aujourd’hui. / Faisons le point sur les chiffres de votre club.

📊

2. Ask about memberships

Your aim: ask clear, short questions about the figures.

Votre objectif : poser des questions courtes et claires sur les chiffres.

You: How many new members are there this week?

Manager: There are eighteen new members.

You: Are there any cancellations?

Manager: Yes, there are four cancellations.

Vous : Combien y a-t-il de nouveaux adhérents cette semaine ? Y a-t-il des résiliations ?

🛠

3. Discuss an issue and action

Your aim: identify a problem and say what happens next.

Votre objectif : identifier un problème et dire quelle sera la prochaine action.

You: Is there any equipment to repair?

Manager: Yes. There is a problem with one treadmill.

You: Please contact maintenance today.

Manager: Yes, I will do that.

Vous : Y a-t-il du matériel à réparer ? / Veuillez contacter la maintenance aujourd’hui.

06 · LILATE READY

Your 45-second professional update

In LILATE, clear and simple language is better than silence. Use this safe structure: your role → your figures → one issue → one action.

Au LILATE, une langue simple et claire vaut mieux que le silence. Utilisez cette structure : votre poste → vos chiffres → un problème → une action.

Exam-style prompt

“Please describe your work and give a short update about one of your fitness clubs.”

  1. Say what you do.
  2. Say how many clubs and staff you manage.
  3. Give two figures.
  4. Say one problem and one action.

Model answer: I am an Operations Manager at Fitness Park. I manage two clubs and work with eleven members of staff. This week, there are eighteen new members at Club A and four cancellations. There is a problem with one treadmill. The manager is contacting maintenance today. Thank you.

Je suis responsable d’exploitation chez Fitness Park. Je gère deux clubs et travaille avec onze salariés. Cette semaine, il y a 18 nouveaux adhérents au Club A et 4 résiliations. Il y a un problème avec un tapis de course. Le manager contacte la maintenance aujourd’hui. Merci.
