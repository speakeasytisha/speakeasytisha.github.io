🧾 CLOE Self‑Introduction Cheat Sheet

SpeakEasyTisha • Frankie • Present yourself (work + location + likes + travel)

🎤 CLOE oral structure (the “safe” plan)

✅ Answer clearly 🧩 Structure 🔗 Use connectors 🙂 Close politely

🧱 6 blocks (30–60 seconds)

  1. 👋 Greeting + name
  2. 📍 Where you live (near city + country + north/south/east/west)
  3. 🏢 Job + employer (what it is)
  4. 🗂️ 2 responsibilities (what you do)
  5. ⭐ Likes (free time) + 1 reason
  6. ✈️ Places visited + short closing
Tip: If you forget a word, rephrase calmly. Fluency = calm + clear structure.

🧠 Grammar you NEED (quick)

  • Present simple (routine): I live, I work, I help, I handle, I like
  • Like + V‑ing: I like walking / I enjoy discovering
  • Present perfect (experience): I’ve visited… / I’ve been to…
  • 1 reason: because / so / to + verb (to relax / to learn)

🧭 Prepositions (location + work)

  • 📍 near + city: I live near Lille.
  • 🌍 in + country: in France
  • 🧭 in the north/south/east/west of: in the north of France
  • 🏢 Employer: work for + organisation: I work for CARSAT.
  • 📑 Field: work in + sector: I work in administration.
  • 📍 Place (office): at the … office: at the CARSAT office

🏛️ How to talk about CARSAT (simple English)

✅ I work for CARSAT 🛡️ social security 🤝 partner organisations

In English, the easiest phrase is:

I work for CARSAT. It’s a social security organisation. I handle files and company documents, answer questions, and coordinate with partner organisations when needed.

Note: Writing: use CARSAT (acronym). Speaking: “CAR‑sat”.

🔗 Connectors (sound fluent)

First of all, Also, For example, However, Overall, To sum up,

✍️ Writing mini‑template (CLOE style)

Subject: Nice to meet you Hello, My name is ____ and I live near ____, in ____. I’m in the ____ of ____. I work in administration for CARSAT as an administrative assistant. I help with documents, appointments, and client requests. In my free time, I like ____. I’ve visited ____. Best regards, ____
Keep it short: 5–8 lines. Use simple, accurate English.

🧩 Ready-to-say models (A2 → B2)

🟦 A2 (simple)

Hello, my name is Frankie. I live near Lille, in France, in the north of France. I work in administration for CARSAT as an administrative assistant. In my free time, I like walking and movies. I have visited Ireland and Ballynahinch. To sum up, I’m organised and I’m happy to speak English.

🟩 B1 (expand)

Hello, my name is Frankie. I currently live near Lille, in France, in the north of France. I work in administration for CARSAT. My job is to help with documents, appointments, and client requests. In my free time, I enjoy walking and discovering new places because it helps me relax. I’ve visited Ireland and Ballynahinch. Overall, I enjoy calm trips close to nature.

🟪 B2 (confident)

Hello, my name is Frankie. I’m based near Lille, in the north of France. I work in administration for CARSAT, a social security organisation. I handle files and company documents, answer questions, and coordinate with partner organisations when needed. Outside of work, I’m interested in walking and discovering new places. I’ve visited Ireland and Ballynahinch. To sum up, I’m organised and I’m motivated to keep improving my English.
