3. Structured grammar for the travel agency

Would like = je voudrais / j’aimerais

Use would like to sound polite when you ask for information, book a ticket, or explain your travel wishes.

Affirmative
I would like to visit Bath.
Negative
I would not like a very crowded city.
Question
Would you like a return ticket?

After would like, use to + verb: I would like to visit London.

Present simple = preferences and facts

Use the present simple for things you like, places that exist, and general travel facts.

I / you / we / they
I like peaceful places.
He / she / it
London has many museums.
Question
Do you like historic cities?

For he / she / it, add -s: Bath looks elegant. London has many museums.

There is / There are = il y a

Use this structure to describe places.

Singular
There is a castle.
Plural
There are many museums.
Question
Are there good restaurants?

Use there is for one thing. Use there are for two or more things.

Formal questions at a travel agency

Use these question starters to sound polite and confident.

Pourriez-vous recommander une destination paisible ?

Je voudrais des renseignements sur les billets de train.

Pourriez-vous me dire la meilleure façon de s’y rendre ?

Serait-il possible de voyager en ferry ?

Before you practise

4. Mini-lessons for every activity

Read these short lessons first. They explain the language you will use in the exercises, dialogue and roleplay.

🧭 Destination description

To describe a place, use: It is + adjective and There is / There are.

Model: It is peaceful. There are museums. There is a castle.

C’est paisible. Il y a des musées. Il y a un château.

🎫 Formal travel requests

At a travel agency, use Could you...?, I would like... and Would it be possible...? to sound polite.

Model: Could you tell me the best way to travel there?

Pourriez-vous me dire la meilleure façon de s’y rendre ?

🚆 Transport language

Use by + transport: by train, by plane, by ferry, by coach, by bus, by taxi.

Model: I would like to travel by ferry and train.

Je voudrais voyager en ferry et en train.

☁️ Weather + time

Use It is + weather adjective and time expressions like next week, in July, for three days.

Model: It is often mild in spring. I would like to stay for three days.

Il fait souvent doux au printemps. Je voudrais rester trois jours.

CLOE preparation

9. Mini oral task

Prepare a 30–45 second answer.

Prompt

Describe your ideal trip to the United Kingdom.

A1 model

I would like to visit Bath. It is calm and elegant. I would like to travel by train. I would like to visit the Roman baths and drink tea in a quiet café.

A2 upgrade

I would like to visit Bath because it looks peaceful, elegant and historic. I would prefer to travel by train because it is comfortable. During my holiday, I would like to visit the Roman baths, walk through the city and relax in a traditional café.
