⭐ Grammar Booster 1 — Past Simple Speaking Review

For VTest and interviews, Angie needs to speak about finished past experiences clearly.

Affirmative

Subject + past verb

I worked at a market.

Negative

Subject + didn’t + base verb

I didn’t work yesterday.

Question

Did + subject + base verb?

Did you work last summer?

WH question

WH + did + subject + base verb?

What did you learn?

QCM — choose the correct sentence

🔗 Grammar Booster 3 — Sequencing words

Sequencing words help Angie speak longer and sound organised.

First

Start your story.

First, I welcomed the customer.

Then

Add the next action.

Then, I answered her question.

After that

Continue the story.

After that, I helped her choose a product.

Finally

Finish the story.

Finally, I thanked her.

Choose the best sequencing word

🎤 VTest Speaking Prompts

Practise speaking for 45–90 seconds. Use the structure: when → where → what happened → what I learned.

Prompt 1

Describe a past work experience.

Last summer, I worked at a market. I helped customers, sold products and answered questions. It helped me become more confident speaking with people.

Prompt 2

Describe a past travel experience.

Last year, I travelled alone. I organised my trip, asked questions and solved small problems. It taught me to be more independent.
