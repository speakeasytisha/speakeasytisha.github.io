1) Essay recipe

Learn the structure before writing

1

Opening sentence

Introduce the topic in a simple and clear way.

Many people believe that good customer service is essential for a positive experience.
2

Opinion / thesis

Give your answer directly so the examiner knows your position.

In my opinion, companies should always respond quickly and politely to customer complaints.
3

Reason 1 + example

Explain your first idea and support it.

First of all, a fast response reassures the customer and shows that the company takes the problem seriously.
4

Reason 2 + detail

Add a second strong idea. Use a connector.

Moreover, polite communication can reduce tension and help solve the issue more efficiently.
5

Conclusion

Restate your opinion and finish neatly.

To conclude, a professional and respectful answer can improve customer trust and protect a company’s reputation.

What gives points

  • Answer the task directly.
  • Write in clear paragraphs.
  • Use connectors naturally.
  • Give reasons and examples.
  • Use precise vocabulary instead of repeating basic words.

What loses points

  • Ideas without explanation.
  • Very short answers.
  • French-style translations in English.
  • Missing connectors or weak conclusion.
  • Repeating the same simple verbs again and again.
