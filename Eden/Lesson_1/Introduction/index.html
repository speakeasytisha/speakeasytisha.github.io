04 · Training method

How the sessions will be built

Different communication skills require different learning methods. The programme will combine structure, practice, repetition, feedback and realistic professional scenarios.

A

Guided practice

A structure or model is introduced first. Then it is applied to your own professional context.

B

Role play

Situations may include project updates, senior management meetings, objections, interruptions and decision-focused presentations.

C

Micro-skills

One specific skill can be isolated: opening a presentation, pausing, recovering attention, answering an objection or closing a message.

D

Recording and playback

When useful, recordings can help you hear rhythm, clarity, pauses, pronunciation and energy variation.

E

Immediate feedback

Feedback will be precise and targeted. The goal is not to correct everything at once, but to improve one or two key elements step by step.

F

Repetition with variation

The same message may be practised in different ways: more concise, calmer, more energetic, more persuasive or more executive-level.

05 · Certification briefing

CLOE: what will be tested

At the end of the training, you will take the CLOE English certification. The work we do will prepare the exam through meaningful professional practice, especially for the oral interview.

Part 1Online adaptive test

Computer-based test

Approximately 50 minutes · 50 questions

This part evaluates your written-language knowledge and comprehension through an online, supervised, adaptive test.

  • Vocabulary
  • Grammar and syntax
  • Expressions
  • Reading comprehension
  • Listening comprehension
Part 2Oral interview

Webcam interview

Approximately 15 to 20 minutes

This part evaluates your ability to communicate orally in a professional or field-specific context.

  • Introduction questions
  • Professional role play
  • Discussion around a professional theme
  • Interaction, pronunciation, fluency and clarity

Result scale

CLOE results are positioned on the CEFR scale: A1, A2, B1, B2, C1 or C2.

Certificate

The certificate is issued from level A2 when the objective set for the training is reached. Otherwise, a results statement is provided.

Preparation strategy

Your CLOE preparation will be integrated into professional speaking practice: role presentation, project explanation, interaction and discussion.

How this training prepares CLOE without becoming an exam-only course

You will practise presenting your role, explaining your professional context, answering questions spontaneously, developing answers without becoming circular and responding to professional situations.

The objective is to prepare the certification through relevant aerospace and project-management communication, not through disconnected exam drills.
