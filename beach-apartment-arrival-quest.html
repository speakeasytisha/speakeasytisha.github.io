3) Grammar for a holiday apartment

Keep the grammar simple, but make it powerful and useful.

Present Simple

facts and general information
  • The apartment has Wi‑Fi.
  • Linen is included.
  • The parking space is behind the building.

Present Continuous

what is happening now
  • I am arriving at the apartment.
  • I am looking for the key box.
  • We are staying for six nights.

Can / Could

ask for help or permission
  • Can I park here?
  • Can you help me?
  • Could you send me the code, please?

Would like

polite information request
  • I would like to know if linen is included.
  • I would like to know where the parking space is.
  • I would like some information about check-in.

⚠️ Watch out

  • ❌ I have reservation → ✅ I have a reservation
  • ❌ Where key box? → ✅ Where is the key box?
  • ❌ I am look for parking → ✅ I am looking for parking
  • ❌ Can you send code? → ✅ Could you send me the code, please?
