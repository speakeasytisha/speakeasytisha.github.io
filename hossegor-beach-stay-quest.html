1) Grammar studio

Learn the grammar you need for this trip.

Present Simple = facts & preferences

Use it for things that are generally true.

Present Continuous = your plan now

Use it for your trip plan or what you are doing now.

Would like = polite booking English

Use it when you ask for something politely.

Grammar contrast

You prefer the beach. general preference
You are travelling to the beach. current plan
You want a hotel near the beach. simple need
You would like to book a room near the beach. polite request

⚠️ Watch out

  • ❌ You are prefer the beach → ✅ You prefer the beach
  • ❌ You booking a hotel → ✅ You are booking a hotel
  • ❌ I want book a room → ✅ I want to book a room
