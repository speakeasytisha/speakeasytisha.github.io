2) Grammar focus: Present simple & Present continuous

🟣 Present simple

Use: routines, facts, schedules

  • I travel in summer.
  • The hotel opens at 2:00 PM.
  • She books trips online.

Keywords: every day, usually, often, on Mondays, in summer

🟣 Present continuous

Use: now, around now, or a current plan

  • I am packing my suitcase now.
  • We are travelling in July.
  • She is calling the hotel.

Keywords: now, today, at the moment, this week

Quick comparison

I travel in August. → general habit / usual plan

I am travelling in August. → a current or arranged plan
