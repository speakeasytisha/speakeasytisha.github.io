3) Grammar workshop: tell the trip clearly

Three tenses, three jobs. Use the mini lessons, then complete the exercises with instant feedback.

Past simple

finished actions

Use: what happened during the trip.

Subject + past verb

We visited Amsterdam. We stayed for four days. We saw windmills.

Past continuous

background / action in progress

Use: what was happening around another event.

was / were + verb-ing

We were walking along the canal when it started to rain.

Present perfect

up until now / life experience

Use: experience before now, result now, or “so far”.

have / has + past participle

I have visited the Netherlands. It has been a memorable trip so far.

Useful final-review structure

  1. Context: I went to Holland for…
  2. Past actions: We visited / saw / stayed…
  3. Story moment: While we were…, something happened.
  4. Present perfect: I have never seen… / I have enjoyed… so far.
  5. Opinion: I would recommend it because…
