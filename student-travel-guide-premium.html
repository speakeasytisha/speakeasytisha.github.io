❓ Step 4

Understanding Questions

Very important for speaking and listening

📍

WHERE

Where do you go?
→ I go to Spain.
🎯

WHAT

What do you do on holiday?
→ I relax. / I visit.
👤

WHO

Who do you travel with?
→ With my family.
📅

WHEN

When do you travel?
→ In summer.
✈️

HOW

How do you travel?
→ By plane.
🔁 Practice tip: Repeat the question first, then answer it.
Example: “Where do you go?” → “I go to Spain.”
🧠 Step 5

Simple Grammar Rules

Present simple vs present continuous

🟢

Present Simple (General)

Pattern: I + verb (like / travel)
“I travel in summer”
“I like the beach”

Use for: general habits and preferences

🔵

Present Continuous (Now / Plan)

Pattern: I am + verb-ing
“I am travelling to Spain”
“I am booking a hotel”

Use for: actions happening now or future plans

🔁 I travel → general habit
⏳ I am travelling → happening now
