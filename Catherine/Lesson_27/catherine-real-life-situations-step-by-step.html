1) The answer formula — how to build your answer

The 4-step formula

1. Answer clearly

Last Friday, I went to court as part of my work as an assessor.

2. Add one important detail

The case involved two very young women.

3. Add an explanation

They had been caught delivering drugs and were in police custody at first.

4. Add a feeling, opinion, or conclusion

It was a difficult case, but the outcome was constructive.

4) Court case speaking lab — step by step

Question 1 — What happened in court last Friday?

Step-by-step answer

Step 1: Last Friday, I went to court as part of my work as an assessor.

Step 2: The case involved two very young women.

Step 3: They had been caught delivering drugs.

Step 4: At first, they were in police custody.

Complete model

Last Friday, I went to court as part of my work as an assessor. The case involved two very young women who had been caught delivering drugs. At first, they were in police custody, and the situation was very serious.

Question 2 — Why was the case difficult?

Step-by-step answer

Step 1: The case was difficult because the young women were very vulnerable.

Step 2: They seemed to have been recruited and manipulated.

Step 3: The situation was emotionally challenging.

Complete model

The case was difficult because the two young women were very vulnerable. They seemed to have been recruited and manipulated, and the situation was deeply concerning. It was emotionally challenging because they were so young.

Question 3 — What support was put in place?

Step-by-step answer

Step 1: The court tried to put support measures in place.

Step 2: One young woman was already in care placement.

Step 3: The other was going to live in supported accommodation.

Step 4: The aim was to support them with health, education, and work.

Complete model

The court tried to put support measures in place. One young woman was already in care placement, and the other was going to live in supported accommodation with regular visits from a social worker. The aim was to support them with health, accommodation, education, and work.

5) Family event speaking lab — step by step

Question — How was your granddaughter’s performance?

Step-by-step answer

Step 1: At the weekend, I went to a dance show for my granddaughter.

Step 2: She is five and a half.

Step 3: The performance took place at a local venue.

Step 4: The costumes were absolutely amazing.

Step 5: I was very proud of her.

Complete model

At the weekend, I went to a dance show for my granddaughter. She is five and a half, and it was a lovely family moment. The performance took place at a local venue, and the costumes were absolutely amazing. I was very proud of her.

7) Writing studio — the same logic in writing

Simple writing recipe

Sentence 1: answer the topic clearly

Sentence 2: add one important detail

Sentence 3: add one explanation

Sentence 4: finish with a feeling, opinion, or conclusion

Model text — court situation

Last Friday, I went to court as part of my work as an assessor. The case involved two very young women who had been caught delivering drugs. At first, they were in police custody, and the situation was very serious. In the end, the court tried to put support measures in place, so the final outcome was more constructive.

Model text — family event

At the weekend, I went to a dance show for my granddaughter. She is five and a half, and it was a lovely family moment. The performance took place at a local venue, and the costumes were absolutely amazing. I was very proud of her.
