3) Syntax lab — English vs French in a simple way

Golden rule 1

English often prefers: Subject + Verb + Object

English: I visited a beautiful Danish town.

French instinct: J'ai visité une ville danoise magnifique.

In English, adjectives usually come before the noun.

Golden rule 2

English often puts place and time near the end.

Example: We had coffee at home in the morning.

Useful order: what happened → where → when

Quick comparison

French-style idea
J'ai beaucoup aimé la maison danoise.
Natural English
I really liked the Danish house.

5) Question café — ask faster, answer more naturally

Question pattern wall

  • Did you…? for past actions
  • Do you…? for routines and habits
  • Are you…? for now and temporary situations
  • Have you…? for life experience / recent result
  • Would you like to…? for polite plans

Useful reactions

  • 😊 That sounds lovely.
  • 🌟 Really? Tell me more.
  • 🤍 That must have been wonderful.
  • 📍 Where did you go exactly?
  • 🕒 What did you do after that?

6) Roleplay pier — a real family-visit conversation

Daughter-in-law: Did you sleep well?
You: Yes, thank you. I slept very well. The house is so calm and comfortable.
Daughter-in-law: What would you like to do today?
You: I would love to walk by the coast and maybe visit a small town or a café.
Daughter-in-law: That sounds lovely. By the way, do you still work with young people?
You: Yes. I work as an assessor with young people in court. I wanted to volunteer at an animal shelter too, but there were already too many volunteers.

Model response you can reuse

Hello! Thank you again for having me. I had a wonderful time here. I really enjoy the coast, the calm atmosphere, and our conversations together. I would love to discover another village today, and later we could have coffee together. By the way, I still work as an assessor with young people in court, and I also wanted to volunteer with animals, but there were too many volunteers at the shelter.

7) Postcard studio — guided writing

Useful building blocks

  • 💙 I had a wonderful time…
  • 🌊 I loved the coast because…
  • ☕ I especially enjoyed…
  • 🤍 Thank you for…
  • 📅 I hope we can… again soon.

Possible model text

Dear both, thank you again for your warm welcome. I had a wonderful time with you in Denmark. I loved staying near the coast and spending time together. The walks, the conversations, and the calm atmosphere were very special. I miss you already and I hope we can meet again soon. With love, Catherine.

Your turn

Write 4 to 6 lines. Include: where you stayed, what you enjoyed, and one feeling.
