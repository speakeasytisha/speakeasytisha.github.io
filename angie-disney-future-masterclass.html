🌟 Dreams, Goals & Professional Qualities Vocabulary

Learn useful interview vocabulary to speak naturally about your future and your strengths.

✨ dream

FR: rêve

A strong personal goal.

My dream is to work at Disney.

🎯 goal

FR: objectif

Something you want to achieve.

My goal is to improve my English.

💪 confident

FR: confiant(e)

Feeling sure of yourself.

I want to feel more confident.

🤝 teamwork

FR: travail d’équipe

Working well with others.

I enjoy teamwork.

😊 friendly

FR: sympathique

Kind and pleasant with people.

I am friendly and helpful.

🌍 experience

FR: expérience

Knowledge from doing something.

I want to gain professional experience.

🚀 opportunity

FR: opportunité

A good chance for the future.

Disney is a great opportunity for me.

🗣️ communication skills

FR: compétences en communication

The ability to communicate with people.

I want to improve my communication skills.

✨ Future Grammar Spell 1 — Going To

Use going to for plans and intentions.

✅ Positive

I am going to work at Disney.

She is going to improve her English.

❌ Negative

I am not going to stop practising.

❓ Questions

Are you going to apply this year?

🎮 Choose the correct answer

I ___ going to apply to Disney.

🎮 Build the sentence

going / I / to / am / practise

✨ Future Grammar Spell 2 — Will

Use will for:

  • quick decisions
  • promises
  • offers
  • predictions
I will help you.
I will check for you.
I think you will love this attraction.

🎮 Choose the best answer

A guest is lost. What do you say?

✨ Like vs Love vs Would Like

Expression Meaning Example
I like normal preference I like Disney movies.
I love strong feeling I love helping people.
I would like professional / future goal I would like to work at Disney.

🎮 Choose the best expression

I ___ work in attractions in the future.

✨ Verb + ING vs Verb + TO

❤️ LOVE + ING

I love reading.

I love helping guests.

I enjoy working with people.

🌟 WOULD LIKE + TO

I would like to work at Disney.

I would like to improve my English.

I would like to work in attractions.

🎮 Correct the sentence

I love cook.

🎤 How to Answer Like a Cast Member

Use this simple structure to sound more natural and professional.

1️⃣ Direct answer

Yes, I would love to work at Disney.

2️⃣ Give a reason

I love helping people and I enjoy the Disney atmosphere.

3️⃣ Future goal

I am going to improve my English and become more confident.

🎭 Disney Interview Question

Why would you like to work at Disney?

🌟 Model Answers — Different Levels

🟢 A1/A2 Model

Hello. My name is Angie. I would like to work at Disney because I love Disney and I like helping people. I am going to improve my English and become more confident.

🟡 A2+/B1 Model

Hello. My name is Angie and I live near Nantes. I would really like to work at Disney because I enjoy helping guests and creating a magical experience for families. I am currently improving my English because I would like to work in attractions or in a boutique. In the future, I would also love to work at Disney World in Orlando.

🟣 B1+ Professional Model

Working at Disney would be an incredible opportunity for me because I am passionate about communication, teamwork, and customer service. I particularly enjoy helping people feel welcome and comfortable. I am highly motivated to continue improving my English so that I can work professionally in attractions or retail environments. In the future, I hope to gain experience at Disneyland Paris before eventually working at Disney World in Orlando.
