📋 1. What Is the VTEST General English Exam?

Four skills

Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking.

Adaptive

The difficulty changes according to your performance and stops when your maximum level is identified.

No pass/fail

You receive a CEFR level for each skill and an overall level for the full four-skill exam.

Important: There is no penalty for a wrong multiple-choice answer. It is better to choose the best answer than to leave a question blank.

⏱ 2. Official Order, Length and Number of Questions

The exact duration changes because the exam is adaptive. Keep up to 140 minutes free; the four-skill test generally lasts approximately 75–140 minutes depending on level.

💻 3. Before the Exam

Online proctoring: Keep your face visible, remain in full-screen mode, do not use another application or external help, and only speak when the test asks you to speak.

🎧 4. Listening Section

Before listening

Read questions and options carefully.

During listening

Listen for the main idea, people, times, places, problems and solutions.

After listening

Answer every question before the timer reaches zero.

The recording plays only once and cannot be replayed. Use elimination and guess when necessary.

📖 5. Reading Section

Skim

Read quickly to understand the main topic.

Scan

Look for names, dates, times, numbers and key words.

Check meaning

Choose the option supported by the text, not by your personal opinion.

Prompt 1 — Describe the Picture

  • where the woman is and what she is doing;
  • how you think she is feeling;
  • what she will do next, and why.
08:00
Words: 0 / 80 minimum

A2: This picture shows a woman in a kitchen. She is cooking food in a pan. She is wearing a striped top and she looks happy. There are vegetables and a bowl near her. I think she enjoys cooking because she is smiling. Next, she will probably put the food on a plate and eat it. She may also prepare the table before the meal. The kitchen looks bright, clean and modern.

B1: This picture shows a woman preparing a meal in a modern kitchen. She is standing next to the cooker and stirring vegetables in a frying pan. She seems relaxed and happy, probably because she enjoys cooking. There are several ingredients and cooking utensils around her. I think she will finish cooking the meal, serve it and perhaps eat with her family or friends. In my opinion, cooking at home can be enjoyable because it is creative and often healthier than buying fast food.

B1+: The picture shows a woman cooking in a bright, modern kitchen. She appears to be preparing a healthy meal with fresh vegetables, and she is stirring the ingredients in a pan. Her smile suggests that she feels relaxed and confident. The kitchen is organised, and everything she needs seems to be close at hand. She will probably finish the dish, serve it and sit down to eat with someone. In my opinion, preparing food at home is rewarding because it allows people to choose fresh ingredients and share a pleasant moment together.

📝 9. Writing Part 3 — Essay Masterclass

What is a VTEST essay?

It is a personal, organised response to a statement or question. The prompt gives several bullet points. Answer every bullet point and write at least the minimum shown on screen.

The SAFE Method

S — Study
Read and underline the bullet points.
A — Arrange
Plan ideas and examples.
F — Form
Write introduction, body and conclusion.
E — Edit
Check spelling, verbs, punctuation and word count.

Simple B1 Essay Structure

Paragraph 1

Introduce the topic and general answer.

Paragraph 2

Reason, explanation and example.

Paragraph 3

Another idea, experience or future plan.

Paragraph 4

Summarise your opinion.

Connector Bank

First of allIn additionHoweverAlthoughFor exampleBecauseThereforeAs a resultOverallIn conclusion

Practice Essay

Working with other people can help us learn and improve.

  • a time when you worked with another person or team;
  • what you learned;
  • how teamwork could help your future career.

Write at least 80 words.

Essay Planner

Words: 0 / 80 minimum

A2: I think teamwork is important. Last year, I worked with other people at Carrefour Market. We helped customers and put products on the shelves. I learned to listen and communicate more clearly. I also learned to ask for help when I did not understand something. In the future, teamwork will help me because I would like to work at Disney. Cast members need to help each other and give guests a good experience. Overall, working with other people can make a job easier and more enjoyable.

B1: I believe that teamwork is very important because people can share ideas and help each other. Last year, I worked at Carrefour Market, where I had to communicate with colleagues and complete different tasks. At first, I was not always confident, but I learned to listen carefully, ask questions and work more efficiently. This experience also taught me that a calm and positive attitude can improve the atmosphere in a team. In the future, teamwork would be especially useful if I worked at Disney because cast members must communicate, solve problems and create positive experiences for guests. Overall, teamwork helps people learn and become more confident.

B1+: Working as part of a team can be extremely beneficial because it allows people to combine their strengths and learn from one another. During my experience at Carrefour Market, I worked with colleagues who had different responsibilities. We needed to communicate clearly, organise our tasks and help customers efficiently. As a result, I became more confident and learned how important it is to listen before reacting. I also discovered that a supportive team can make difficult situations easier to manage. In my future career, particularly if I have the opportunity to work at Disney, teamwork would be essential because cast members must cooperate to ensure safety and create memorable experiences for international guests. In conclusion, teamwork improves both professional skills and confidence.
