🪄 Chapter 3 — Tense Review Rules

Past Simple

Finished past actions.

I worked at Carrefour.

yesterday, last year, ago

Present Simple

Routines, facts, likes, qualities.

I enjoy teamwork.

every day, usually, often

Present Continuous

Now / temporary progress.

I am improving my English.

now, today, currently

Going to

Future plans.

I am going to prepare for my interview.

next year, soon

Would like to

Polite dreams and goals.

I would like to work at Disney.

Easy → choose the correct tense

Medium → choose the best verb form

Hard → correct the mixed tense mistake
