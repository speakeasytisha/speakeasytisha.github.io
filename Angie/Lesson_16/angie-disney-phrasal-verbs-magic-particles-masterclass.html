🧠 What is a phrasal verb?

A phrasal verb is a verb + a small word. The small word often changes the meaning.

look

Use your eyes.

Look at the map.

look for

Try to find.

I am looking for the parade.

look after

Take care of.

I look after guests.

look into

Investigate.

I will look into the problem.

Important: Do not translate word by word. Learn the whole expression.

🎯 Verb + Preposition Combinations

Essential for natural English and VTEST accuracy.

listen to

écouter

I listen to the guest carefully.

wait for

attendre

Please wait for a moment.

look for

chercher

I am looking for the ticket office.

apply for

postuler à

I would like to apply for a job at Disney.

talk about

parler de

I can talk about my experience.

speak to

parler à

May I speak to a supervisor?

depend on

dépendre de

It depends on the situation.

arrive at / in

arriver à / en

I arrive at the station. I arrive in France.

🎙 Voice Memo Challenge

Use Voice Memos on iPhone/iPad. Record yourself, listen again, and compare with the models.

Prompt 1

Use look for, find out and give back.

A2: I will look for the wallet and give it back.

B1: If a guest loses a wallet, I would look for it, find out where they last saw it and give it back if we find it.

B1+: If a guest reported a lost wallet, I would first find out where they last saw it, then look for it carefully and give it back following the park procedure.

Prompt 2

Use run out of, check out and look into.

A2: We ran out of this size. I can check another shop.

B1: If we run out of a size, I can check out another boutique or look into another solution.

B1+: If we have run out of the size a guest wants, I would check out the stock in another boutique and look into the best solution for them.

Prompt 3

Use interested in, good at and ready for.

A2: I am interested in Disney. I am good at helping people.

B1: I am interested in working at Disney because I am good at helping customers and I am ready for a new challenge.

B1+: I am very interested in working at Disney because I am good at communicating with people, and I feel ready for a customer-service role in an international environment.

✍ Writing Lab

Lost item message

Write a message using: look for, find out, give back.

I will look for the lost item and find out where the guest last saw it. If we find it, we will give it back following the correct procedure.

Interview answer

Write an answer using: interested in, good at, proud of, ready for.

I am interested in working at Disney because I am good at helping customers. I am proud of my progress in English, and I feel ready for the VTEST and for a new professional challenge.
