Part C — Reading Comprehension

Text: Hello team, tomorrow the park will be very busy because of a special event. Please arrive 15 minutes early, check your uniform, and welcome guests with a smile. If a guest asks for directions, show them on the map. If there is a problem, contact your supervisor immediately.

Part C2 — Listening Comprehension

Listen, then answer the questions.

Excuse me, I bought this sweatshirt yesterday, but it is too small. I have the receipt. Could I exchange it for a larger size, please?

Part D — Writing Task

Task: A guest writes that an attraction was closed after a long wait. Write a polite answer with an apology, explanation and solution.

A2: Dear guest, I am sorry. The attraction was closed. You can try another attraction.

B1: Dear guest, I am sorry for the inconvenience. The attraction was temporarily closed for safety reasons. You could try another attraction nearby.

B1+: Dear guest, thank you for your message. I understand your frustration, and I am sorry for the inconvenience. The attraction was temporarily closed for safety reasons. On your next visit, you could check the app for wait times or ask a cast member for recommendations nearby.

Part D2 — Speaking Task

Task: Prepare your spoken answer: Tell me about yourself, your experience and your future goals.

A2: My name is Angie. I am motivated and friendly. I have worked with customers. In the future, I would like to work at Disney.

B1: My name is Angie. I have worked with customers before, especially at Carrefour Market. I am improving my English because I would like to work at Disney in the future.

B1+: My name is Angie. I am motivated, patient and passionate about customer service. My past experiences have helped me become more confident with customers, and I am currently improving my English. In the future, I would love to work at Disney and create memorable experiences for international guests.
