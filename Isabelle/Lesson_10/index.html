Step 3

🧭 Grammar patterns for cover letters

Each pattern is followed by practice so you can use it actively, not just read it.

1. Apply for + position

Rule: Use apply for + the position / the role / the job.

✅ I am writing to apply for the position of Senior Real Estate Transaction Manager.

✅ I would like to apply for the role of Real Estate Transaction Manager.

✅ I am interested in the real estate position advertised on your website.

Avoid: I apply to the job / I apply as real estate.

2. Work in / work as / position of

Rule: Use the correct structure depending on the meaning.

work in + field
I would like to work in real estate.
work as + job
I would like to work as a real estate consultant.
position of + job title
I am applying for the position of Real Estate Consultant.

3. With over 24 years’ experience

Rule: Use with over + number + years’ experience in + field.

✅ With over 24 years’ experience in real estate, I have developed strong legal and commercial expertise.

✅ I have over 24 years’ experience in the French property sector.

4. Experienced in / skilled in + noun or -ing

Rule: After experienced in and skilled in, use a noun or a verb ending in -ing.

✅ Experienced in contract negotiation.

✅ Experienced in negotiating lease-related documentation.

✅ Skilled in risk analysis and client relations.

5. I would welcome the opportunity to…

Rule: This is a polite, professional closing phrase for a cover letter.

✅ I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience could support your team.

✅ I would be pleased to discuss my application with you in more detail.

Step 7

📄 Final model cover letter

This is a model, not something to memorize. The goal is to understand the structure and reuse the best phrases.

Subject: Application – Senior Real Estate Transaction Manager

Dear Hiring Team,

I am writing to apply for the position of Senior Real Estate Transaction Manager at Just Eat Takeaway.com. I am particularly interested in this role because it combines real estate transaction strategy, lease negotiation, stakeholder coordination and international portfolio work.

I am a real estate legal and commercial professional with over 24 years’ experience in the French property sector. My background combines legal advisory work for major real estate developers, property development expertise and sales management responsibilities. In my previous roles, I drafted and negotiated real estate development contracts, analysed risks, supported operational teams and liaised with external partners including notaries, lawyers, investors and landlords.

Your role requires strong negotiation skills, lease-related knowledge and the ability to coordinate with landlords, brokers, legal teams, finance teams and workplace stakeholders. I believe my experience in contract negotiation, transaction support, client relations and risk prevention could allow me to contribute effectively to your real estate objectives.

I am now seeking a new professional opportunity in the Netherlands and would be pleased to bring my legal and commercial expertise to an international real estate environment. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience could support your team.

Kind regards,
Isabelle Davion
