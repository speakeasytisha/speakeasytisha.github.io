Step 7

🔎 CV correction workshop: sentence upgrades

These are not “mistakes to blame”. They are professional CV upgrades that make your profile clearer internationally.

Profile sentence structure
Before

Real estate legal and commercial professional with over 24 years’ experience..., I am looking for...

Professional

Real estate legal and commercial professional with over 24 years’ experience... Now seeking a new professional opportunity...

Rule: CV profiles often use short professional phrases, not long personal sentences.

Advised operational teams
Before

Advised operational team on real estate law...

Professional

Advised operational teams on real estate law, urban planning, construction and environmental matters.

Rule: use plural teams when you mean several teams or departments.

Reviewed legal documents
Before

Reviewed and negotiated acts: sales promises, land acquisition acts...

Professional

Reviewed and negotiated legal deeds and property documents, including preliminary sale agreements, land acquisition deeds, easements and co-ownership regulations.

Rule: “legal documents” or “legal deeds” sounds more natural than “acts” in this CV context.

Litigation and disputes
Before

Drafted settlement agreements Monitored building permits appeals Monitored disputes and provisions

Professional

Drafted settlement agreements and monitored building permit appeals, disputes and related legal provisions.

Rule: separate ideas clearly. One bullet can contain 2–3 linked actions, but punctuation must be clean.

Skills section
Before

Adapation and reactivity · Rigor

Professional

Adaptability and responsiveness · Attention to detail

Rule: in English, “attention to detail” is more natural than “rigor” for a CV.

Step 9

🗣️ Speak from your CV: interview and CLOE practice

Practise answering from prompts first. Then reveal a model answer.

1. Can you introduce yourself professionally?

Use: real estate legal and commercial professional · 24 years · property developers · Netherlands

I am a real estate legal and commercial professional with over 24 years’ experience in the French property sector. I have worked for major property developers, combining legal advisory work, contract negotiation and sales management. I am now looking for a new opportunity in the Netherlands, ideally in a real estate role where I can use both my legal and commercial experience.

2. What is your main strength?

Use: legal expertise · commercial insight · listen carefully · client needs

My main strength is my ability to combine legal expertise with commercial insight. I understand real estate transactions from both a legal and a client-focused perspective. I also listen carefully to clients and partners before speaking, so that I can understand their needs and offer appropriate advice.

3. How does your experience match this role?

Use: contracts · leases · transactions · stakeholders · risk

My experience matches this type of role because I have worked on real estate contracts, lease-related documents, transaction support and risk analysis. I have also coordinated with notaries, lawyers, investors, landlords and operational teams, which is important in international real estate transaction management.

4. Why do you want to work in the Netherlands?

Use: new challenge · Amsterdam · integration · international environment

I would like to work in the Netherlands because I am looking for a new professional and personal challenge. I would like to build my life in or around Amsterdam and integrate into the Dutch community. Working in an international real estate environment would allow me to use my experience while continuing to develop my English and my knowledge of the local market.

5. What can you bring to an international real estate team?

Use: experience · negotiation · legal awareness · stakeholder coordination

I can bring strong real estate experience, legal awareness and commercial understanding. I am used to negotiating contracts, analysing risks, working with different stakeholders and supporting operational teams. I can also bring a careful, client-focused approach and a strong motivation to work in an international environment.

6. Tell me about your sales management experience.

Use: property assets · clients · negotiations · annual budget · team

As a sales manager, I managed the sale and marketing of various property assets across France. I organised calls for tenders, client visits and negotiations, and I managed an annual fees budget of approximately one million euros excluding VAT. I also coordinated a small sales team and worked with a wide range of clients and external partners.
