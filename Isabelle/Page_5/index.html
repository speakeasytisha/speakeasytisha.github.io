🎤 Professional profile structure

  1. Background: I have worked in real estate for 24 years.
  2. Double expertise: I combine legal expertise with commercial experience.
  3. Sales: I have experience in selling different types of properties.
  4. Legal: I can analyse risks and give legal advice.
  5. Client approach: I listen carefully before speaking.
  6. Goal: I would like to work in or around Amsterdam.

I have worked in real estate for 24 years, combining legal expertise with commercial experience. I have experience in selling different types of properties and working with a wide range of clients. I also have a strong legal background, which helps me analyse risks and give appropriate advice. I listen carefully to clients before speaking so that I can understand their expectations and recommend the right solution. I would now like to use this experience in an international real estate environment in or around Amsterdam.

✍️ Task: 100–130 words

Write a short professional profile for a real estate agency in the Netherlands.

Include:

  • your years of experience
  • your legal and commercial background
  • one sales achievement or responsibility
  • your client communication style
  • your goal in or around Amsterdam
  • one reason English is important for your project

Model beginning:

I am a French real estate professional with 24 years of experience, combining legal expertise with commercial responsibilities. I have worked with property developers and clients across France.
