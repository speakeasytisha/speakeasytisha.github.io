Step 2

Paragraph-by-paragraph upgrade path

Use these pathways as a guided comparison. The objective is not to memorise every line. It is to understand why professional English becomes more precise and easier for a recruiter to read.

Your starting idea

“I am particularly interested in this role because it combines real estate transaction business strategy, stakeholder coordination and international portfolio work.”

Clearer English

I am particularly interested in this role because it combines real estate transactions, business strategy, stakeholder coordination and portfolio work in an international context.

Targeted professional version

I am particularly drawn to this role because it combines real estate transactions, strategic legal advice, investor-facing work and cross-functional stakeholder coordination.

Why this is stronger: English tends to list parallel nouns clearly: transactions, strategy, coordination and portfolio work. The targeted version also uses language that matches the role without copying the advertisement word for word.
Your starting idea

“My background combines legal advisory work for major real estate developers, property development expertise and sales management responsibilities.”

Clearer English

My background combines legal advisory work for major real estate developers with property development expertise and sales management experience.

Targeted professional version

Over 24 years, I have combined legal advisory work for major property developers with hands-on commercial experience in sales management, negotiations and client relations.

Why this is stronger: experience is often more natural than responsibilities in a profile paragraph. The targeted version adds concrete transferable strengths without creating a long list.
Your starting idea

“I believe my experience in contract negotiation, transaction support, client relations and risk prevention could allow me to contribute effectively to your real estate objectives.”

Clearer English

I believe that my experience in contract negotiation, transaction support, client relations and risk prevention would enable me to contribute effectively to your real estate objectives.

Targeted professional version

I would bring a practical, business-oriented approach to contract negotiation, risk assessment and coordination across internal teams and external stakeholders.

Why this is stronger: would enable me to is a polished way to explain potential contribution. The targeted version leads with the value you bring, which feels more confident.
Your starting idea

“I am aware of the need to master Dutch. I have abilities for learning languages. Under certain conditions, I could benefit from free Dutch lessons…”

Clearer English

I understand that professional Dutch is important for this role. I am currently learning Dutch and I am committed to developing the language skills needed to work effectively in the Netherlands.

Targeted professional version

I understand that fluency in Dutch and English is central to this position. I am actively developing my Dutch and would bring strong English communication skills, international motivation and a disciplined approach to professional language learning.

Why this is stronger: The paragraph stays honest, positive and professional. It focuses on commitment and readiness, rather than administrative arrangements.

Step 4

Grammar patterns for credible cover letters

Each pattern gives you a professional structure you can reuse in future applications.

01

Apply for + the position of + role

Use apply for when you ask to be considered for a vacancy.

I am writing to apply for the position of Manager Legal & Business Affairs at Holland Immo Group.
02

Experience in / experience with

Use experience in for an area or activity; use experience with for people, tools or specific processes.

Experience in contract negotiation · experience in managing transactions · experience with external advisers

Choose the most natural version:

03

Would enable me to / would allow me to

Use these structures to show how your background could contribute to the employer’s goals.

My experience in transaction support would enable me to contribute effectively to your team.
04

Parallel lists: keep the structure even

When you list skills, use the same grammar pattern for each item.

contract negotiation, risk assessment, stakeholder coordination and investor communication

Step 5

Vocabulary bank: Holland Immo language

Open each category and keep the expressions that you can use honestly in writing and speaking.

⚖️ Transactions and legal work +

transaction

FR: transaction immobilière

A deal involving the acquisition, financing, leasing or disposal of a property or asset.

Example: I have supported complex real estate transactions.

due diligence

FR: audit préalable / vérifications préalables

A detailed review of legal, financial and commercial information before a transaction.

Example: I can contribute to legal due diligence and risk assessment.

disposal

FR: cession

The sale of a property or investment asset.

Example: The role includes acquisitions, financing and disposals.

off-plan lease

FR: BEFA / bail en l’état futur d’achèvement

A lease signed before the premises are completed.

Example: I negotiated off-plan lease documentation for a senior residence project.
📊 Business, investment and reporting +

portfolio

FR: portefeuille

A collection of properties or investments managed by an organisation.

Example: Portfolio management requires both legal and commercial awareness.

investor relations

FR: relations investisseurs

Communication and relationship management with investors.

Example: I am interested in developing my experience in investor relations.

reporting

FR: reporting / communication d’informations

Providing clear and accurate information to management or investors.

Example: Transparent reporting supports sound decision-making.

business-oriented

FR: orienté business

Focused on practical commercial outcomes as well as technical accuracy.

Example: I bring a pragmatic and business-oriented approach.
🤝 People, coordination and contribution +

stakeholder

FR: partie prenante

A person or group affected by, involved in or able to influence a project.

Example: I coordinate with internal teams and external stakeholders.

external adviser

FR: conseil externe

An outside professional who provides specialist advice.

Example: I have worked closely with external advisers, including notaries and lawyers.

risk mitigation

FR: réduction des risques

Actions that reduce the likelihood or impact of a risk.

Example: My legal work has focused on risk identification and mitigation.

contribution

FR: contribution / valeur ajoutée

What you bring to a team, project or organisation.

Example: I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my contribution to your team.

Step 7

Make your Dutch-language point sound professional

Use this part with care: it should be honest, direct and focused on your plan to develop the language skills required for the role.

B1

Clear

I am learning Dutch and I am motivated to improve quickly.

B1+

More professional

I am currently learning Dutch and I am committed to developing the professional language skills needed to work effectively in the Netherlands.

B2 target

Role-focused

I understand that fluency in Dutch and English is central to this position. I am actively developing my Dutch and I am committed to reaching the professional level required for an in-house role in the Netherlands.

Useful cultural point: Do not over-explain financial support, lesson funding or programme conditions in the letter. In a first application, recruiters mainly need to understand your current level, your honest plan and your professional motivation.

Step 10

Speak from your letter: interview and CLOE practice

Your letter can become a strong spoken introduction. Start with the cue, then reveal a model only after you have tried.

Question 1

Tell me about yourself and why you are interested in this role.

Cue: 24 years · legal + commercial · transactions · Netherlands

I am a real estate legal and commercial professional with over 24 years of experience in the French property sector. My background combines legal advisory work for major property developers with sales management, contract negotiation and stakeholder coordination. I am interested in this role because it brings together real estate transactions, strategic legal advice and a business-oriented perspective. I am now looking to build the next stage of my career in the Netherlands.

Question 2

What could you bring to Holland Immo Group?

Cue: practical · risk · contracts · people · commercial view

I would bring a practical and business-oriented approach to legal and commercial real estate matters. I am used to assessing risks, drafting and negotiating contracts, and coordinating with a wide range of stakeholders. My commercial background also helps me understand the client and business perspective behind a transaction.

Question 3

The role requires fluent Dutch. How are you preparing for this?

Cue: honest · active learning · professional level · commitment

I understand that Dutch is essential for this role. I am currently learning Dutch and I am actively building the professional language skills I would need to work effectively in the Netherlands. I am committed to continuing this learning alongside my professional transition, while contributing through my English communication skills and my real estate experience.
