6) Grammar (polite requests · comparatives/superlatives · past tense)

Short rules + exercises with instant feedback.

Polite requests

  • I’d like…
  • We’d like…
  • Could I have…?
  • Could you…?
Example: Could you recommend a quiet restaurant, please?

Comparatives & superlatives

Comparative (2 things): cheaper / more expensive / better / more comfortable
Superlative (#1): the cheapest / the most expensive / the best
Example: This hotel is cheaper, but that hotel is more comfortable.
