3) Micro‑Lesson — The “timeline trio”

News needs a timeline. This trio keeps your story clear.

Present Simple

General / now facts, routines, headline style

“Markets react quickly to uncertainty.”

Past Simple

Finished yesterday / last week / in 2026

“The government survived the vote.”

Past Continuous

Background while / when / as

“While prices were rising, people were worrying.”

Pick a tense + topic, then build a sentence. Tap 🔊 to hear it.
